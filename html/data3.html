Introduction Netco Technology Pvt. Ltd. (NETCOTECH) in Nepal was established in year 2003 with vision to gear towards placing Nepal in the Global Market by focusing on quality education & Training, Overseas Educational Consultancy including Global IT Certification courses, and software development, Since its inception, it is planning towards working in partnership with the corporate sector, while retaining its IT solutions freedom and placing NETCOTECH as a Total Software, Hardware Solution Provider and Education Consultants Along with that NETCOTECH is an Authorized Prometric Testing center, Microsoft Gold Partner, Red hat Authorized Training Partner, Mission Our mission is to become a leading IT solution provider, trainer and Educational Consultants with quality services to achieve total customer satisfaction in national and international marketplace. Moreover, it includes assisting all the trainees seeking IT education to attain their personal, educational and career goals using the latest technology means in the shortest time, affordable fees and the most effective manner. The aim is not only to educate but also to provide a proper foundation that will help to equip for work as professionals. We have a cadre of professional trainers, application developers, experienced abroad counselor that have the in-depth specialized experience and knowledge in the field of Information Technology and overseas study . We strive to be an organization of excellence to our customers, partners, fellow colleagues and society. Location Netco Technology Pvt. Ltd. (NETCOTECH) Head office is located at Adwaitmarg in Putalisadak-31 way to Shanker Dev Campus from Old Bus Park,behind LAW Campus, Central part of the Capital with Total physical area of 4000 square feet with ample of space for both the Training and Parking Purposes as well. Which is one of the most popular commercial centres in Kathmandu Moreover, the place where the NetcoTech is located is calm and peaceful for getting education, in terms of infrastructures and services. Strategy Our Strategy is to establish ourselves as a global solution provider in the information technology and consultancy service market aiming for excellence. We shall accomplish this through application of technology, motivation and continuous improvement of our knowledge and skill. Further plan is to expand our branches in different major cities like Pokhara, Butwal, Nepaljung, Mahendranagar, Birjung and Biratnagar within a year, which proves that NETCOTECH, has a strong presence all over NEPAL with a network of offices located in all the major cities of Nepal Training Netco Technology conducts different training courses with fully equipped laboratory along with multimedia projector. Apart from being a computer training institute Netco Technology is an authorized Prometric Testing Center, Microsoft Certified Gold Partner, Redhat Training Partner, so is facilitated with the separate laboratory for separate faculty i.e. for test preparation, for MCSE training, for RHCE training. For full time courses and other individual course along with CISCO training, Netco Technology has six Laboratories each containing 10-15 computers. Abroad Study Established in 2003, Netco Technology (NETCOTECH) Pvt. Ltd. is one of the leading overseas education consultants in NEPAL representing over 150 world premier Universities around the world. For the past 2-3 years, we have been sending hundreds of students every year to various highly reputed Universities/ Colleges in UK, Ireland, USA, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, Switzerland, The Netherlands, Singapore and Japan. l NETCOTECH head office located at Putalisadak-31 KTM which is one of the most popular commercial centre in Kathmandu and branch offices located in Balaju, Baneshwor, and Lalitpur, This has enabled us to reach out to a much wider population of the student market and provide them our services in the fields of career counseling, visa assistance, travel assistance and admissions at the Undergraduate Graduate and Postgraduate levels. NETCOTECH has been dedicated to the cause of providing personalised consultancy to students as well as parents, at all stages of studying abroad, from School to University level and have sent more than hundred students to Universities/ Colleges of UK every year in the past. NETCOTECH offers a unique range of services dedicated to advising parents and school at all stages of education abroad from pre-school to post-university. Each year we receive hundreds of enquiries from students for overseas study. Most of our clients contact us after personal recommendations from friends and relatives who are familiar with our services; others are reffered to us by other advisory sevices, international organizations or diplomatic representatives. Every student has individual requirements, but in order to ensure a well-informed choice there are almost always special factors to consider, including a student's academic background, age, knowledge of English, when to come to the university or college, the length of the student's stay in the university/college and what, where and how he/she wishes to study. We assist students with all aspects of planning and arranging for their study overseas. OUR LOCATION Netco Technology Pvt. Ltd.
