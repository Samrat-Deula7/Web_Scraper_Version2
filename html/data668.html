Visa Application & Support

At Global Peace Education Academy, we provide complete visa application support to our students. From the very first step of preparing documents to ensuring a smooth visa approval process, our team guides you with care and expertise. Our support doesn’t stop there—we continue to assist even after you begin your journey abroad, ensuring you feel secure and confident throughout.

Test Preparation

Global Peace Education Academy offers comprehensive test preparation classes for Japanese and Korean language proficiency exams. We provide training for JFT, NAT, JLPT (Japanese-Language Proficiency Test), TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean), and other essential exams. Our experienced instructors ensure students are well-prepared to achieve the best possible results.

Documentation Guidance

We provide clear, step-by-step guidance on all required documentation so students never feel lost or confused. From application forms to supporting papers, our experts ensure every detail is accurate and complete. With our thorough approach, we cover all aspects of your study-abroad documentation process, making it hassle-free and reliable.

Placement in Schools and Colleges

We help students get placed in their preferred schools, colleges, and universities in Japan and Korea. Global Peace connects you to institutions in your chosen city and also provides better recommendations if your selected options are not available. Our strong partnerships with reputed institutions allow us to offer students the most suitable academic pathways.

Counseling & Career Guidance

At Global Peace, we believe that every student’s journey is unique. Our professional counselors provide personalized academic and career counseling to help you choose the right course, institution, and future path. With our honest advice and experienced insights, students can make informed decisions about their education and career abroad.

Student Screening

To ensure the success and eligibility of our students, we conduct student screening sessions before processing applications. This helps us evaluate academic background, financial capacity, and long-term goals, ensuring each student is well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities of studying abroad.

Message from the CEO - Global Peace

It gives me great pleasure to share the journey of Global Peace with you.

Back in 2004, I traveled to Tokyo, Japan, on a student visa. There, I studied the Japanese language and, after graduation, pursued Hotel Management at a vocational college for two years. Following my graduation, I gained valuable experience working in the hotel industry for a few years.

With the skills and knowledge I acquired abroad, I returned to Nepal with a dream — to create opportunities for students who aspire to learn languages and study overseas. That dream gave birth to Global Peace in the Eastern region of Nepal.

Today, Global Peace proudly provides quality education in Japanese and Korean languages, along with computer courses and study-abroad services. I am delighted to announce that we have successfully expanded our branches to Urlabari,  Itahari and Damak, and we are committed to extending our services to various other cities in the near future.

At Global Peace, our mission is to empower students with skills, knowledge, and confidence to achieve their goals and succeed globally.

Thank you for your trust and continued support. Together, we will keep growing and creating brighter futures.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How long does the application process typically take?

    The application process generally takes 8–14 months, depending on the type of program, document readiness, and intake schedule. This includes school selection, language test preparation, document submission, Certificate of Eligibility (COE) processing, and visa application.

    What countries do you help students apply to?

    We assist students in applying to Japan and Korea, providing end-to-end support for university, vocational, and language programs in these countries.

    Can you help with scholarship applications?

    Yes. We guide students through various scholarship opportunities available in Japan and Korea, including government scholarships, university-based scholarships, and private grants, helping them prepare all necessary documents and applications.

    What is the processing time?

    Processing times vary based on school acceptance, COE issuance, and visa approval. Typically, school acceptance and COE take 6–8 weeks, while visa processing at the Embassy/Consulate can take 1–3 weeks after COE submission.

    What will be the duration of the visa?

    The duration of a student visa depends on the program length. Language schools usually issue visas for 6 months to 2 years, vocational schools and university programs may issue visas for 1–4 years, renewable as long as the student maintains enrollment and meets visa conditions.
