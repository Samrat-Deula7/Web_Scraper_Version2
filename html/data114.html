Start Your Study abroad journey

Embarking on your study abroad journey with GIEC International Education means receiving comprehensive support from start to finish.

We guide you through every step, from test preparation and course selection to visa applications and pre-departure information. Our expert team ensures a smooth and stress-free process, making your dream of studying abroad a reality.

  • Test Preparation

    Preparing for tests is crucial for studying abroad. GIEC provides personalized coaching to help you excel in IELTS, TOEFL, and SAT exams. Our experienced tutors offer tailored strategies and resources, ensuring you achieve the scores needed for your desired course and destination.

  • Course Selection

    Choosing the right course is essential for your future career. At GIEC, we offer expert guidance to help you select the course that matches your interests and goals. Our counselors provide detailed information on various programs and universities, ensuring you make an informed decision.

  • Documentation

    Proper documentation is vital for your study abroad application. GIEC assists you in organizing and preparing all necessary documents, including transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal statements. We ensure all paperwork is accurate and complete, streamlining your application process.

  • Admissions

    Navigating the admission process can be complex. GIEC simplifies this for you by providing step-by-step guidance. We help you with application forms, deadlines, and submission requirements, ensuring your application is timely and well-prepared, increasing your chances of acceptance.

  • Financial Preparation

    Understanding financial requirements is crucial for studying abroad. GIEC provides detailed information on tuition fees, living expenses, and potential scholarships. We guide you in preparing a financial plan that covers all costs, ensuring you are financially ready for your study abroad experience.

  • Visa Application

    Obtaining a student visa is a critical step in your journey. GIEC offers comprehensive support in preparing and submitting your visa application. Our experts guide you through the process, ensuring you meet all requirements and increasing the likelihood of a successful visa approval.

  • Insurance

    Having proper insurance is essential for studying abroad. GIEC assists you in selecting the appropriate health and travel insurance plans, ensuring you are covered for any unforeseen circumstances. We provide information on various options, helping you choose the best plan for your needs.

  • Pre-Departure

    GIEC provides detailed pre-departure information before you leave. We offer guidance on travel arrangements, accommodation, and cultural adaptation. Our goal is to ensure you are well-prepared for your new life abroad and make your transition as smooth as possible.
