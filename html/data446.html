Expert Advice & FAQ

Everything you need to know about studying abroad, visa processes, and life in global destinations.

You must demonstrate that you have sufficient funds to cover your tuition and living expenses for at least the first year of study (as shown on your I-20 form). Generally, this ranges from $30,000 to $60,000 depending on the university and location.

For most undergraduate courses in Australia, a minimum IELTS score of 6.0 with no band less than 5.5 is required. For postgraduate courses, an overall score of 6.5 with no band less than 6.0 is typically expected. However, requirements vary by university and level of study.

Yes, international students on a Student Visa in the UK can typically work up to 20 hours per week during term time and full-time during vacations, provided they are enrolled in a full-time degree-level course.

To apply for a Canada student visa from Nepal, you need an acceptance letter from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI), proof of funds to cover tuition and living expenses, and a valid passport. Transit Education guides you through the entire SDS and non-SDS application process.

If you are applying for an English-taught program, German language proficiency is not strictly mandatory for admission. However, basic German (A1/A2) is highly recommended for daily life and is often required for internships and part-time jobs.
