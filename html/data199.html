Company History

Motif Education Abroad Pvt Ltd

With the primary objective of empowering Nepalese students to be internationally competent through global education, Motif Education Abroad (P) Ltd (formerly: Motif Language & Abroad Studies (P) Ltd) was established in 2007 A.D. by young Nepalese entrepreneurs, who are also the promoters of Nepal’s leading academic institutions.

What we do?

With the primary objective of empowering Nepalese students to be internationally competent through global education, Motif Education Abroad (P) Ltd (formerly: Motif Language & Abroad Studies (P) Ltd) was established in 2007 A.D. by young Nepalese entrepreneurs, who are also the promoters of Nepal’s leading academic institutions.

Strategy, Vision & Values

Empowering Dreams, Expanding Horizons. Our mission is to provide exceptional study abroad opportunities and guidance, while our vision is to be the leading global education consultancy committed to student success. We uphold a stringent quality policy, ensuring the highest standards of service delivery and personalized support to our students throughout their academic journey.

Vision

To empower students with world-class education opportunities, fostering global citizens who will make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Mission

Our hardworking professionals are dedicated full-time as education counsellors and International Admissions Office of our partners to provide authentic information to students.

Quality Policy

Authentic, free and informative counselling to the students so as to help them find the right study destination and colleges/universities.

Core Values

Our values reflect who we are and what we stand for as industry leaders of a nation.

Commitment

Teamwork

Respect

Will to Succeed
