Welcome to West­ern Ed­u­ca­tional Ser­vices

West­ern Ed­u­ca­tional Ser­vices (WES) is controlled by pro­fes­sional, ded­i­cated, well ex­pe­ri­enced, and aca­d­e­mi­cians to pro­vide the ser­vices for higher ed­u­ca­tion in dif­fer­ent coun­tries of abroad. It is for the most part directed by the prin­ci­ple of trust and in­no­va­tion. It has been giv­ing ser­vices to Nepalese stu­dents by pro­vid­ing stan­dard­ized test prepa­ra­tion classes like: IELTS, TOEFL, SAT, GRE and GMAT.

The primary ob­jec­tive of our in­sti­tu­tion is to be the one stop ed­u­ca­tion cen­ter for stu­dents will­ing to concentrate abroad. We give the per­fect and pro­fes­sional coun­sel­ing for choos­ing the streams, se­lec­tion pf the right col­leges and uni­ver­si­ties with af­ford­able expense. We be­lieve in per­son­al­ized care and de­liv­ery of high stan­dard and au­then­tic ser­vice. We pro­vide the moment de­tails of every single reality which makes different in the ap­pli­ca­tion process of the stu­dents. This will re­sult to qual­ity of ser­vice than the quan­ti­ties of­fered.

We en­sure the stu­dents to lead life with right de­ci­sion to en­roll in abroad col­lege/uni­ver­si­ties. We sup­port students to pick the ap­pro­pri­ate col­leges/uni­ver­si­ties with schol­ar­ship/fel­low­ship and awards.
