Who We Are ?

Gurukul Education provides quality, career-focused learning programs including Japanese and Korean language classes, IELTS & PTE preparation, entrance preparation courses, tuition classes, and computer & IT training. Our goal is to support students in improving their academic performance, language skills, and technical knowledge through practical and result-oriented teaching methods. We are committed to delivering updated course content, expert guidance, and a supportive learning environment to help students achieve their educational and professional goals. At Gurukul Education, we focus on building strong foundations and essential skills needed for future success.
