How to Study Abroad from Nepal

📌

Research and Choose Your Destination

Identify your preferred country, university, and program based on your academic goals, career aspirations, and budget.

📝

Prepare Required Documents

Gather academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores (IELTS, PTE, etc.), a Statement of Purpose (SOP), financial proof, and other necessary documents.

✅

Apply to Universities & Secure Admission

Submit your applications to selected institutions, and once accepted, obtain the Confirmation of Enrollment (CoE) or offer letter.

✈️

Apply for a Student Visa

Complete the visa application process with required documents, financial proof, and health insurance, and prepare for your pre-departure journey.
