Frequently Asked
Questions ?

  • 01. What services does EDEN Academy Consultancy offer ?
    We provide career counseling, documentation guidance, legal services, mock interviews, Statement of Purpose (SOP) assistance, study abroad support, and test preparation for exams like IELTS, GRE, TOEFL, and more.
  • 02.How can EDEN Academy help with visa applications?
    Our team offers expert guidance on visa types, requirements, and documentation for student, dependent, and visitor visas. We assist with the entire application process to ensure a smooth and successful outcome.
  • 03. Which countries and institutions can I apply to through EDEN Academy?
    We have connections with top universities and colleges in countries including Australia, Canada, South Korea, the USA, the UK, Europe, India, and New Zealand. We help you explore options and find the best fit for your academic goals.
  • 04. What are the benefits of studying abroad with EDEN Academy?
    Studying abroad with EDEN Academy provides you with valuable international experience, exposure to diverse cultures, and a competitive edge in the job market. Our personalized support and extensive network ensure a successful and rewarding educational journey.
