About Us

Alliant International Education is registered to Ministry of Industry, Nepal Government with registered no . # 123456. We dispel all the myths associated with studying abroad and brought the concept of overseas education to the doorsteps of every student by making the procedures simple, convenient and updated . The academic and professional excellence of our company is primarily rooted in its human resources. Our counseling members include alumni from Australia and US Colleges and universities and are Certified USATC and QEAC professionals. We take legitimate pride in our team that processes a judicious blend of rich experience and updated knowledge to operate internationally. The Department heads and seniors with international experience & exposure and highly qualified professionals provide the expert guidance to all the candidates.
