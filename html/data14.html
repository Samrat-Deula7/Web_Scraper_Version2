Biography

Jyangjee Kharel is a Registered Migration Agent at the Rockdale office of Expert Education and Visa Services, with four years of experience in the migration industry. He has developed strong expertise across a wide range of migration matters and is committed to providing practical, reliable, and client-focused migration advice.

Before entering the migration profession, Jyangjee worked in the Energy and Environment sector. His experience in this field helped him build strong analytical, communication, and regulatory understanding skills. Driven by his interest in migration law, he later transitioned into the migration industry to help individuals and families achieve their goals in Australia.

Jyangjee completed the Graduate Diploma in Migration Law and Practice from the University of Technology Sydney. As a Registered Migration Agent, he assists clients with various visa pathways and skills assessments. He is known for his professional approach, clear communication, and dedication to supporting clients throughout their migration journey.
