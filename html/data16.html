TARA INTERNATIONAL EDUCATION AT A GLANCE

Tara International Education Pvt. Ltd is an education consultancy located at Aradhana Complex, Bagbazar, Kathmandu, Nepal. It is a professional organization accredited by the reputed universities and colleges, and professional awarding and governing bodies to provide first hand information about the higher education opportunities in abroad. We represent a number of leading universities and colleges in various countries like Australia, New Zealand, UK, USA, Canada, Japan, India and Ireland. Since our establishment around eleven years, we have been appreciated as one of the highly trusted and principled organizations in Nepal. Our mission is to be an effective bridge between the universities abroad and students to provide genuine information on the higher education opportunities abroad. Keeping this in mind, our main business activities at the moment are:
  • Abroad Study Counselling for the countries like Australia, NZ, UK, USA, Canada and Ireland
  • IELTS Preparation Classes
  • ToEFL Preparation Classes
  • SAT Preparation Classes
  • Language Preparation Classes—English
  • Visa Interview Preparation Classes
  • Student Visa Documentation and Guidelines
Last but not least, this company strongly believes in providing honest, up-to-date, genuine and accurate information with friendly and courteous service. Our academic counsellors are highly qualified in handling the students’ matters right from the university admission process till the visa process. They take utmost care to get 100% successful results. We understand the requirements of the students in terms of their previous qualifications, family status, financial background, future career goals and a combination of such factors. Our academic advisors make sure that students choose the right course as well as destination. We are fully committed to ensuring that students receive a worthwhile, productive and high quality experience abroad.
