Why Oli & Associates for Abroad Study?

As a top education consultancy in Nepal since 2002, Oli & Associates has helped over 30,000 students achieve their dreams to study abroad with a high visa success rate for eligible applicants and greater client satisfaction. Our team consists of 155 professionals, including 50 QEAC-certified counselors, who provide expert guidance for studying in Australia, Canada, the UK, the USA, and New Zealand, ensuring your journey to international education is smooth and successful.

We’ve helped 50,000+ students score band 8 or higher in IELTS/PTE, maintain strong partnerships with globally recognized universities, and operate offices across Nepal, Australia, and Bhutan for complete support from application to arrival. With over 100 awards for excellence and a commitment to honest, transparent advice, the top education consultancy in Nepal is your trusted partner for studying abroad.
