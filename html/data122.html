What We Offer?

Chiba Education Center (CEC) is a trusted consultancy dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying in Japan. With a focus on personalized guidance, the center offers comprehensive services including counselling to help students choose the right course and institution, documentation assistance to ensure all paperwork is accurate and complete, and visa processing to streamline the application process.

In addition to academic and visa services, Chiba Education Center also offers Japanese language classes to help students build essential communication skills and adapt to life in Japan. CEC also assist with travel arrangements, ensuring a smooth transition from Nepal to Japan. Whether it's academic planning, language preparation, or logistical support, Chiba Education Center is committed to empowering students and confidence they need to succeed in their educational journey.
