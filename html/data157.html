Services

TRANSLATION SERVICES

We provide translation services for the documents from Japanese to English; English to Japanese; Nepali to Japanese ; Japanese to Nepali. We attest the documents with official stamping and the translation are widely accepted.

JAPANESE LANGUAGE CLASSES

We conduct Japanese Language Classes starting from N5 to N4 level with word meaning books and other educational materials. We have professional teachers all with N1 or N2 level of Japanese Language Proficiency Test (JLPT).

EXAM PREPARATION CLASSES

We provide JLPT/JLCT/NAT exam preparation classes as a requirement for entering to Japan with a student or working Visa.

INTERVIEW PREPARATION CLASSES

We have setup a special course for those wishing to attend interview for student, part-time or working visas.

ORIENTATION CLASSES

Before departing for Japan, we provide a comprehensive orientation to ensure students are fully prepared for their new life and studies. Our guidance includes learning how to write a proper biodata in Japanese, how to search and apply for jobs, and the correct way to contact job sites. We also train students on essential interview skills—how to speak confidently, present themselves professionally, and communicate effectively during interviews—helping them step into Japan with confidence and readiness.

COUNSELLING FOR STUDY IN JAPAN

We provide professional and student-focused counselling services for those aspiring to study in Japan. Our experienced counsellors guide students at every step—from choosing the right course and institution to visa preparation and pre-departure orientation—ensuring a smooth and confident journey toward Japanese education.

Affiliated Colleges

AICHI INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY

Aichi International Academy accepts international students with a variety of needs, and through effective Japanese language education based on language acquisition theory, aims to improve their Japanese language ability while also providing them with the knowledge and skills necessary for cross-cultural understanding, further education, employment, and life in Japan.

ARIST FOREIGN LANGAUGE SCHOOL

At Aristo Foreign Language Academy, we are open to people from overseas who wish to learn Japanese, and we offer a thorough curriculum to help as many people as possible master the language.

Westcoast Language School

West Coast Language Academyis a school with a long history, established in December 1989. Although it is located in Tokyo, it is quiet, easy to live in, and very convenient for daily life. We have received rave reviews for our detailed support for studying and living in Japan, which is the key to a successful study abroad experience.
