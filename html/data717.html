    Welcome To
    Dolphin Education Study Centre Pvt. Ltd.

    Dolphin Education Study Centre Pvt. Ltd. was conceived and founded to offer quality education and educational services to its student benefactors who aspire to succeed academically in a global academic sphere. Since its inception,
    Dolphin Education Study Centre's goal has always been to enhance the native educational standards and produce competent human resources who can make significant contributions to their land besides fashioning for themselves their future.
    In order to achieve this goal, we are relentlessly working towards building educational partnerships with universities and colleges abroad, so we can give concrete shapes to our cause: guaranteeing a privilege of internationally recognized education to all Nepalese students. Our goal of providing internationally recognised education could be materialized in doing so.
