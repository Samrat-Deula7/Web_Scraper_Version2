Consultancy

Education first was established in 2008. Since its inception, EF has placed hundreds of students abroad at various countries including USA/UK/Australia/Canada/New Zealand and Europe. EF is an admission consulting expert which provides premium consulting services to students who aspire and want to study abroad. We have been able to place our students at some of the world’s leading Universities such as Carnegie Mellon University, Duke University, London School of Economics, Brunel University London, ISB, INSEAD, Babson College, The University of Melbourne, Wake Forest University, Ohio State University, Australian National University, IE University Spain etc.

Jamboree: Jamboree Education is the oldest institute in India in the field of comprehensive classroom preparation programs for tests such as GMAT, GRE, SAT, TOEFL and IELTS. Each center follows a uniform curriculum and has outstanding faculty, all of whom are trained at our head office. All of the centers offer the same teaching standards and syllabus. What distinguishes Jamboree Education and sets it apart is its expertise in test preparation.

 About the MD

In a nation, where lack of awareness and understanding among targeted beneficiaries regarding the importance of quality education has resulted in a huge vulnerable population, Mr. Manoj's foundation has been working towards improving this situation and filling this gap. He strongly believes that learning and inspiring goes parallel and has consistently taken that extra leap to positively impact the community through the field of education. He is the Managing Director of Euro School, Chhauni which is a technologically equipped co-educational school that provides quality learning experience. He is also the Founder and Managing Director of Education First, and the franchise partner of Jamboree, India. Besides this, he has been actively involved in NAAER and IERIN for the past 6 years and is currently serving as a President of IERIN.
