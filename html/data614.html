1. What is Eduport

Eduport offers online tuition for Class 7 to 12 students (State & CBSE syllabus) and NEET/JEE coaching, taught by alumni from prestigious institutions like NIT, IIT, and AIIMS. The platform focuses on personalized learning through live classes, video lessons, and regular exams.

2. Which courses are available on Eduport?

Eduport provides online courses for SSLC, Plus One, Plus Two (Science, Commerce), NEET, JEE, and KEAM exam preparation. Courses are structured to align with the school curriculum, ensuring students are well-prepared for board and entrance exams.

3. Who teaches the courses at Eduport?

Eduport courses are taught by experienced tutors who are graduates from renowned institutions such as NIT, IIT, and AIIMS. They ensure a strong conceptual understanding, supported by motivating study tips and exam strategies.

4. Is Eduport suitable for NEET/JEE preparation?

Yes, Eduport offers specialized courses for NEET and JEE aspirants. These courses are designed by experts and focus on both board exam preparation and entrance exam success.
