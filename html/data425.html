YOUR TRUSTED PARTNER IN GLOBAL EDUCATION

Forbid Education Career is a trusted and government-authorized education consultancy based in Bagbazar, Kathmandu, Nepal. We are committed to guiding ambitious Nepali students toward world-class academic and career opportunities across the globe.

With a student-first approach and a team of experienced counselors, we provide personalized guidance in university selection, course matching, test preparation, visa processing, and much more. Whether you’re aiming for the USA, UK, Australia, Japan, South Korea or other top destinations, we ensure a smooth and successful journey from Nepal to your dream university abroad.

Our strong network of international university partners, ethical practices, and a proven track record of student success make us one of the most reliable and sought-after consultancies in Nepal.

At Forbid Edu Career, your dream to study abroad is not just a goal it’s our mission.

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-Wishers,

Since our founding in 2013 A.D., Forbid Education Career Pvt. Ltd. has grown into one of Nepal’s most trusted, government-authorized education consultancies, headquartered at Bagbazar, Kathmandu. We now serve students from multiple cities—including Kathmandu, Pokhara, Butwal, Dang, Palpa, Nepalgunj, Banepa, Dhangadhi, Surkhet, Devdaha, and Rampur—through companyowned and managed offices across the country.

At Forbid, our core mission is to support Nepali students in fulfilling their study abroad goals by offering ethical, personalized, and high-quality guidance. We assist them in accessing global academic and career pathways, providing end-to-end support—from choosing the right university to handling visa applications.

As the Managing Director, I am dedicated to ensuring our organization delivers trusted, student-centered services. Our experienced, government-accredited counselors offer expert advice on education, migration, test preparation (IELTS, PTE, JLPT/NAT/JPT), and essential services to help students move forward with confidence.

We believe every student should have the chance to explore international opportunities. With a passionate and professional team, we are committed to guiding each student through their journey, turning aspirations into achievements. Your success is our greatest reward.

- AMMAN KC
