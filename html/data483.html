In 3 easy steps

Our Sajilo Processing

Complete Processing

Get in Touch & Plan Your Journey

The first step is to connect with us! Whether via phone, email, or our website, we'll schedule a consultation to understand your academic goals, preferred study destinations, and visa requirements. Our expert consultants will guide you in choosing the right university and program that aligns with your career aspirations. We’ll also provide information about scholarships and financial options, ensuring you make informed decisions from the start.

Prepare & Submit Your Applications

Once you’ve decided on your course and university, we assist you in preparing all the necessary documents—transcripts, letters of recommendation, test scores, and personal statements. We’ll carefully guide you through the entire application process to ensure that everything is complete and meets the university’s requirements. Additionally, we help you with the visa application process, from document submission to interview preparation, making sure your applications are processed smoothly and promptly.

Post-Arrival Support & Transition

After you’ve secured your visa and arrived at your study destination, we offer ongoing support to help you settle in. Our services include assistance with accommodation arrangements, airport pick-up, and an orientation to help you navigate your new environment. Whether it’s adjusting to a new culture, understanding local systems, or getting settled into your university, we ensure that your transition is as smooth and stress-free as possible.
