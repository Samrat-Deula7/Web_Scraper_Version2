📢 New Work Visa Options Announced for International Graduates in New Zealand

🔺 Immigration New Zealand has announced new work visa options for graduating international students as part of the International Education Going for Growth Plan.

🔺 Key updates expected in late 2026 include:

👉 Introduction of a 6-month Short-term Graduate Work Visa for eligible NZQCF Level 5–7 graduates who do not qualify for a Post-Study Work Visa.

👉 Post-Study Work Visa eligibility extended to New Zealand Graduate Diploma holders who already hold a bachelor’s degree (including degrees obtained overseas).

🔺 These changes aim to strengthen New Zealand’s ability to attract, retain, and transition international graduates into the workforce, while maintaining the quality and integrity of the international education system.

🔺 For students considering New Zealand, this reinforces the importance of planning a clear study → work → residence strategy.
