      About Us

      Koizora International Language Academy

      It’s our pleasure to welcome you to Koizora international language academy. It is located at Bagbazar, Kathmandu, Nepal. Our office is also member of education consultancy association of Nepal and JALSAN (Japan Language School Association of Nepal). we are also approved consultancy from ministry of education of Nepal. we provide professional support to those who plan to study in Japan.
      • We offer wide variety of Japanese and cultural classes for students.
      • We provide the bridge between the culture of Japan and Nepal.
