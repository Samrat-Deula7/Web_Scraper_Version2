FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What services does Lincoln Int Edu Foundation provide?1

    We provide Academic Counseling, Visa Assistance, Documentation Guidance, Mock Interview Practice and College Interview Practice for students planning to study abroad.

  • Which countries can I apply to?2

    You can apply to UK, Canada, Australia, Malta, France, Denmark and other European countries through Lincoln

  • Do you provide Visa Mock Interview Practice?3

    Yes, we conduct realistic mock interview sessions to prepare students for actual embassy and college interviews with confidence.

  • Which test preparation courses do you offer?4

    We offer coaching for IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo, PTE, SAT, GRE, GMAT and TOPIK examinations.

  • Is your consultancy service free?5

    Visit our office at Bagbazar, Kathmandu or Ghorahi, Dang for a free initial consultation session.

  • What documents are needed for student visa?6

    You need transcripts, passport, bank statements, SOP, recommendation letters and admission letter. Our team guides you completely.

  • How long does visa processing take?7

    Visa processing generally takes 4 to 12 weeks depending on the country and embassy requirements.

  • Why choose Lincoln?8

    Lincoln has 17 plus years of experience since 2007 with expert team of 10 professionals serving students nationwide.

  • Can I apply without IELTS, PTE or any English language test?9

    Yes, you can apply without IELTS or PTE in France, Malta, UK and many more countries. We will guide you.
