ABOUT US

Education and Migration Corner
Empowering Education and Career Paths

Education and Migration Corner (EMC) is a leading professional consulting company specializing in abroad study and career counselling. Founded in 2013, EMC is committed to assisting individuals in realizing their dream of relocating or pursuing education overseas.

With a team of skilled advisors and experts, EMC provides personalized attention, guidance, and the resources necessary for academic success. We are dedicated to creating solutions tailored to each student's unique needs.

  • Skilled advisors specializing in diverse services
  • Strong educational relationships worldwide
  • Up-to-date with industry trends and opportunities

Our Mission

Our mission is to guide individuals in their educational and migration journeys with exceptional support. Through a comprehensive range of services, we help people navigate study abroad and immigration pathways to Australia, Canada, the UK, the USA, and Europe. Each journey is designed to open doors to a successful and rewarding future.

02

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading destination for students and professionals seeking world-class education and global opportunities. We strive to be a supportive partner, empowering individuals to achieve their aspirations while fostering international collaboration and understanding through education and migration.
