Become a Homestay

Home / Accommodation / Become a Homestay

Homestay parents are a crucial component to the international student experience. At RIE, we support our homestay parents to provide students with a warm, safe and welcoming home to develop English, learn a new culture and embark on a new adventure.


RIE Homestay Requirements

  • Provide a clean private room with bed, dresser, desk and window.
  • Ensure an English speaking environment
  • Ability to provide 3 meals a day plus snacks
  • Undergo a Criminal Record Check
  • Willingness to include an international student into your family and provide a friendly environment for a student new to Canada. 

Become a Homestay

Get the process started by completing our online application today.

For more information on becoming a homestay parent, contact us at study@sd38.bc.ca
