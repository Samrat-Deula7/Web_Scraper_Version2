About Us

Welcome to Ajisai Nepal Education Foundation

AJISAI Nepal Educational Foundation is a Japan-based institution established in 2010 A.D. Our primary goal is to provide high-quality language education and effective counseling services to individuals, groups, and companies in Nepal. We believe that language is the key to communication, and at AJISAI, we focus on using communicative and direct teaching methods to help our students and business personnel develop the necessary skills to communicate confidently in Japanese.  With a strong emphasis on increasing fluency and accuracy, we aim to equip our clients with sound language skills.
