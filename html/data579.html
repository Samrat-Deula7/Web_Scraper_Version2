About Himalayan Education Services Private Limited

Himalayan Educational Services Pvt Ltd (HESPL) is a non-government service-based enterprise is engaged in and carries out the following activities:

  • Manages and runs educational institute schools, universities, distance & online education, vocational training, skill development courses, and organizes competitive examinations, etc.
  • Carries out the business of imparting educational consultancy services and conducts vocational, skill development training programmes and certification courses.
  • Designs, develops, maintains, organizes, conducts, promotes and provides services, trainings, coaching programs, self- evaluation test in and outside of India through online and e-learning in subjects like management, commerce, business, statistics, accountancy, information technology, medical, legal, engineering, data processing, communication and other technical subjects.
