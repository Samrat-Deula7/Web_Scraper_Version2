A Little About Us

Globalization has led people from different backgrounds, culture and language to gather in one place and peaceful integrity has become rules in those places where people from various backgrounds have made their effort to understand & share their common interest through social interactions, seminars & most importantly through education. Keeping this in mind, Ohayou Company has been established to bridge the gap between Nepal and Japan by providing language & cultural education services.

The current trend of students going abroad for better education & career is still on the peak due to the social unrest and inferior education quality within the home country. Many students abroad face the same dilemmas on the road to the right decision due to the lack of information & counseling from the concerned resource & experts.

Ohayou was established solely with the purpose to provide Japanese language education, familiarize students with the history & culture of Japan and guide them for further study in Japan through appropriate facilities such as text materials & expert advice. Many of the students who have received counseling from our counselor, who has the first hand experience of the Japanese culture and their educational system, are standing at the forefront of their careers.

Their smart choice & counseling from us has led them not only to become a good model for their career development but they have succeeded in contributing to the global peace through better understanding of Japanese culture & coexisting with other foreign nationals in Japan via education. This experience & education shall of course! contribute to deeply understand & develop their home country after their return. We assure each and every individual willing to study in Japan, a qualitative basis Japanese education & fruitful consultation which shall act as an eternal asset for their futures ahead.
