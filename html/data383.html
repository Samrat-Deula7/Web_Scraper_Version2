WHY CHOOSE US

Educity International Education Pvt. Ltd., established in 2015, is a trusted education and career consultancy offering professional and reliable counseling to Nepalese students aspiring to study abroad. Led by experienced young professionals, Educity specializes in soliciting, counseling, instructing, and academics.

We guide students from the first stage to final.

 

✅ Selection of College & Universties   ✅ Accommodation & Travel Arrangement

✅ Formatting Documents                      ✅ Interview Preparation
