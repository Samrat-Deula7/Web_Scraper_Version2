About Us

Meet the team!

DECADES OF EXPERTISE,
DEDICATED TO YOU

Ashford International PVT LTD is a registered and Nepal Government-approved education consultancy based in Putalisadak, Kathmandu. Since 2009, we have been committed to guiding students toward global academic opportunities with integrity and expertise.

Our team of experienced counselors—certified through QEAC, New Zealand training programs, and TITI—provides reliable, up-to-date, and personalized advice to help aspirants make informed decisions about their educational futures.

THE FOUNDER

Our consultancy was founded by Ms. Namuna Pudasaini, a dedicated education expert with over a decade of experience guiding students toward global academic success. With a strong background in international education and a passion for student empowerment, he established this consultancy to bridge the gap between ambition and opportunity. Under her leadership, the company has grown to become one of the most trusted names in Putalisadak, known for ethical counseling, high visa success rates, and personalized support.

OUR VISIONS

01.

To be a trusted global partner for students, guiding them towards academic excellence and successful international careers.

02.

To create opportunities for students to access world-class education while building a future of growth, knowledge, and innovation.

03.

To empower students with accurate information and personalized counseling to pursue education across borders.

04.

To establish a trusted platform where students receive transparent, reliable, and result-driven guidance for their study abroad journey.

05.

To shape future leaders by connecting students with quality education, diverse cultures, and global career opportunities.
