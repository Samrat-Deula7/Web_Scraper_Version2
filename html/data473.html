Frequently Asked Question

Have Any Queries?

Any Queries related to Iron Gate and Study Abroad.

  • Is there an age limit on claiming my child as a dependent?

    there are specific criteria to determine whether you can claim your child as a dependent for tax purposes. While there is no specific age limit, certain conditions must be met. Generally, to claim your child as a dependent, they must be under the age of 19 at the end of the tax year, or under 24 if they are a full-time student.

  • What challenges should i expect to face?

    When venturing into new experiences, it’s important to anticipate certain challenges that may arise. While everyone’s journey is unique, some common challenges include cultural adaptation, language barriers, academic adjustments, homesickness, financial management, social integration, health and wellness, and navigating bureaucratic processes. By acknowledging these potential obstacles, you can better prepare yourself mentally and emotionally to overcome them.

  • What funding is available?

    There are several funding options available for study abroad, including scholarships, grants, financial aid, study abroad program funding, work-study programs, crowdfunding, and personal savings. These resources can help cover tuition fees, accommodation, travel expenses, and living costs during your study abroad journey.

  • Can i Work during my studies Abroad?

    Yes, in many cases, international students are allowed to work during their studies abroad. However, the regulations and restrictions regarding work opportunities can vary depending on the country and the specific visa or residence permit you hold.
