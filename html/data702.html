Welcome To

Tyoyo Education Consultancy

We are here to guide and support you on your journey to obtaining a recognized and rewarding academic degree. At Tyoyo, we understand that education is not one size fits all. That's why we cater to students based on their situation, needs, and career objectives.

Established by a team of experts with years of knowledge in the field of education, both locally and globally, we always place the interests of our students first. We provide personalized services and career paths to students wishing to study in Australia, USA, UK and Canada.
