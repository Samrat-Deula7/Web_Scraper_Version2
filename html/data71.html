Welcome to Pacific International Consultancy

At Pacific International Consultancy, we are dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad while offering comprehensive language courses to prepare them for global opportunities. Based in Nepal, our consultancy provides expert guidance and personalized support to students aiming to pursue higher education in international universities. Whether you’re looking to further your education abroad or improve your language skills, Pacific International Consultancy is here to ensure your success with our experienced team and vast network of educational institutions worldwide. Let us help you turn your aspirations into reality!

Our Services

Study Abroad Consultation

Our expert consultants provide personalized support to help you navigate the complexities of studying abroad. From visa assistance to finding scholarships and processing applications, we ensure a smooth transition to your chosen international institution.

Test Preparation​

Achieving the right score on language proficiency tests is crucial for international study. We offer professional test preparation courses for exams like IELTS and TOEFL, helping you secure the scores needed for your university admissions.​

Academic Counseling​

We provide one-on-one academic counseling to help students select the right courses and universities that align with their career goals. Our team ensures that each student’s educational path is well planned and supported with the right information.​​

Training/Classes​

We understand that interviews are a key part of securing admissions and scholarships. Our specialized interview preparation classes equip you with the skills and confidence to excel in both university interviews and visa interviews.​

Why Choose Pacific International ?

At Pacific International Consultancy, we pride ourselves on having an experienced team that guides you every step of the way, ensuring your educational journey abroad is as smooth as possible. Our personalized counseling approach focuses on your unique goals, offering tailored advice from course selection to visa support. With an extensive network of top universities and institutions worldwide, we provide access to a wide range of study options to help you find the perfect fit. Most importantly, our 100% visa success rate reflects our commitment to making your dream of studying abroad a reality, with a meticulous process that guarantees the best possible outcomes.
