About

Paurakhi Education

It is not only that we send students to Japan at Paurakhi Education Consultancy — we build dreams, establish futures, and guide every step of the way with love, passion, and expertise.

With nearly 12 years of track record, Paurakhi has proudly seen hundreds of students realize their dream of studying in Japan. We were founded on a mission to empower Nepali students, and now we are a trusted Nepal-Japan bridge — built on trust, commitment, and history.

Our team of passionate counselors and experienced advisors works tirelessly to make your journey to Japan clear, smooth, and full of opportunity. From choosing the right course and mastering the Japanese language to handling documentation and securing your student visa — we’re by your side.

And that's not all we have to offer. Our support extends beyond. We make you ready for life in Japan with pre-departure orientation, cultural counseling, and post-arrival support — because your success overseas begins with the proper support at home.

If you dream of language school, vocational training, or university studies — Paurakhi is your ally to success. We value straightforward advice, one-on-one guidance, and extended support that continues even after you arrive in Japan.
