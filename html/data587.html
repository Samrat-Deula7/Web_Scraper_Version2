Welcome To Third Eye Education

Established with the aim of delivering quality educational services, Third Eye Education have more than a decade long history of serving students for further studies in Australia, New Zealand, UK, USA and Canada. We also provide preparation classes of IELTS, PTE, GMAT, TOEFL and SAT.

Third Eye Research Education Consultancy Pvt. Ltd is Nepal based education consultancy which provides professional consulting services to students in Nepal who like to pursue further education in other countries. Having the best consulting team with their prior knowledge in working with foreign universities and also the requirements of Nepalese students can correlate and give proper advice on the exact location and course to be taken up. Being a total solution based and a flawless education consultancy, Global Opportunities can surely show you a right way to a bright future.
