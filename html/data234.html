About

Value Education & Visa Services (VEVS Global Nepal) offers assistance with visa processes and extensions, including the option to have a team member accompany you to the immigration department if needed. They also facilitate entry visas upon arrival in Kathmandu TIA immigration. Clients have praised their knowledgeable and professional team for their excellent handling of the visa process, expressing gratitude for their quick and efficient services.
