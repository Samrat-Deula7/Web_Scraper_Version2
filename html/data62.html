    Why Choose Edwise Foundation?

    Here are some reasons why we are the preferred institution among students seeking test preparation courses and consultation to study in the U.S., Canada, and other top destinations. Our reputation as a trusted consultancy in Nepal is built on our commitment to excellence and our comprehensive support services, making us the top choice for those looking to study abroad.

    Proven Guidance on Financial Aids

    Our Advisors are extremely careful in scrutinizing the students’ potentials, profiles, financial constraints, future aspiration, and interests and in exploring possible options.

    Right Fit Approach in Consulting
    Our consulting services are driven by a ‘right-match’ philosophy which has resulted into the overall success of students, and a stable relationship with the institutions.
    Smart Assistance with F-1 Interview
    We support student clients physically (also virtually on request) on standardized testing, all aspects of admission process ranging from sort-listing universities to application process, essay writing tricks and visa application process relating to U.S. universities and abroad.
    1-1 Personalized Counseling
    We have very informed and amicable Advisors who enthusiastically guide the students from the day they step in Edwise to the day before their departure to their preferred destination. Our advisors also keep in touch with students after they reached in the U.S. and aboard, just in case they need some assistance.
    Proper Environment for the Students to Prepare for the Tests
    We strive to provide the best academic and social environment for each individual student seeking to study in the United States and other study destinations through accurate, ethical and high quality services.
