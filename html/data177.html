Test Preparation

Understandably, English is a secondary Language for Nepalese students. Excel education offers test preparation classes for aspiring students.

Proof of English language competency is compulsory for all students who want to pursue higher studies in English-speaking countries. To prove your competency in language, you need to score on Language tests such as TOEFL, IELTS, and PTE. We offer classes to help you score the maximum.

Students need to achieve at least the minimum requirements set by the college/university.

Similarly, SAT, GMAT, and GRE tests are required by various universities to check the student’s proficiency levels. Therefore, we offer our students SAT, GMAT, and GRE classes.
