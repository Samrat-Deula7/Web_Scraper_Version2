Our Story

Founded in 2020, Edu‑Mark India was born to bridge the gap between education and the digital era. While many schools struggled with online adaptation, branding, and student engagement — we stepped in not just as marketers, but as educators, creators, and enablers of knowledge.

From helping small schools improve digital literacy to guiding universities on storytelling, our mission is rooted in the belief that education must evolve — and we must help it do so, meaningfully.
