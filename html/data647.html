Pathshala is ready to make your pathway clear and success
How can I apply for the Pathshala Nepal Foundation?

Applying to Pathshala is easy! You have two options: Apply online through a convenient application form on our website (https://apply.pathshala.edu.np/) or visit the school administration office in person either in Baneshwor or Bagdol to submit your application.

What types of programs does Pathshala Nepal Foundation offer?

Pathshala Nepal Foundation offers a comprehensive educational journey! For younger students, we provide programs specifically designed for grades 1 through 10, building a strong foundation for future learning. As students progress, Pathshala supports their academic goals further with pre-university programs for grades 11 and 12.

How can I apply to Pathshala?

Pathshala offers two convenient ways to apply:

  • In Person: You can Visit the school administration office at either our New Baneshwor or Bagdol in Kathmandu.
  • Online: Download the application form from our website  Downloads

For either option, be sure to submit the completed application form along with all necessary supporting documents within the specified timeframe.

Who is the CEO of Pathshala School?

Ishwor Adhikari is the founder and CEO of Pathshala Nepal Foundation, which was established in 2004 as a co-educational English medium school.

Where is Pathshala located?

Pathshala Nepal Foundation has schools in two locations within Kathmandu, Nepal:

  • New Baneshwor
  • Bagdol, Lalitpur
Why is the Pathshala the best school in Kathmandu?

Pathshala is considered one of the best schools in Kathmandu because of the following reasons: 

  • Focus beyond academics
  • Interactive learning
  • Smaller class sizes
  • Extracurricular opportunities
