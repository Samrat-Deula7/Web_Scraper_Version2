We help Making your dream into Reality

Google Educational Link is a reputed and well-known educational consultancy that provides counseling for studying in abroad. It also provides the facilities for learning different languages. The company is registered under the existing laws of the Nepal government and it provides IELTS, PTE, and Duolingo language training and also conducts student exchange programs between Nepal and other developed countries. We are undoubtedly a leading institution in Nepal that addresses the needs of those students who aspire to study in abroad to further their ambition of higher education in top universities.
