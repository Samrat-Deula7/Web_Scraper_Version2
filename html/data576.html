About Us

UniGlobal International Educational Foundation is a pioneer in the field of Overseas Education and Immigration. Established in 2015, as a Private Limited Company under the Government of Nepal, Ministry of Education our primary aim is in assisting competent youngsters achieve academic heights along with immigration by anchoring them in exemplary Overseas Educational Institutions at the right time at the lowest cost UniGlobal International Educational Foundation excels in its aim through the conviction and hard work of our staff at the network of offices at New Baneshwor Kathmandu Nepal.
