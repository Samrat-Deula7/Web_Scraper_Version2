Consultancy

Founded in 1994 AD, Professional Education Consultancy takes pride as a pioneer in the industry. We have successfully guided thousands of students and helped them go abroad for their higher education.  

The process of enrolling and helping students identify a particular college/university that best suits their interest and skill has never been easy. However, our team of professional counselors have been successfully guiding and helping students delightfully reach their destinations. We coach all of our students and provide them with every required assistance for a successful and fruitful transition.  

Maintaining professionalism and ensuring ethical operations are most important to us. Our expertise in helping students plan their journey towards their success is how we best help them prepare for a better and brighter future. With over 27 years of experience, we combine creativity and innovation, to help students' confidently move ahead towards their academic pursuits for assured international enrollments. We have also recently been paying great attention to our Corporate Social Responsibility as we want to sincerely help and contribute to the betterment of our society and help those in need.
