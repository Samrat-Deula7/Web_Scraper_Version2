      About WIEC

      WI Education Consultancy is a government-licensed educational consulting firm registered under the Ministry of Company Registrar, Nepal. Since our establishment in 2015, we have been dedicated to providing expert guidance in overseas education and immigration services. Our experienced and specialized team has successfully assisted hundreds of students in pursuing their higher education abroad, helping them choose the right courses, universities, and countries that align with their academic and career goals. Located in Putalisadak, Kathmandu — Nepal’s central educational hub — WI Education is at the heart of where Nepal’s top academic institutions and international education service providers are based. We take pride in offering personalized counseling and updated information on globally recognized programs affiliated with renowned universities both at home and overseas. Whether you're planning to study in the USA, Australia, UK, Canada, or Europe, WI Education is your trusted partner in shaping a brighter academic and professional future abroad.

About Us

Your Pathway to

Global Education

WI Education Consultancy is a government-licensed educational consulting firm registered under the Ministry of Company Registrar, Nepal. Since our establishment in 2015, we have been dedicated to providing expert guidance in overseas education and immigration services. Our experienced and specialized team has successfully assisted hundreds of students in pursuing their higher education abroad, helping them choose the right courses, universities, and countries that align with their academic and career goals. Located in Putalisadak, Kathmandu — Nepal’s central educational hub — WI Education is at the heart of where Nepal’s top academic institutions and international education service providers are based. We take pride in offering personalized counseling and updated information on globally recognized programs affiliated with renowned universities both at home and overseas. Whether you're planning to study in the USA, Australia, UK, Canada, or Europe, WI Education is your trusted partner in shaping a brighter academic and professional future abroad.
