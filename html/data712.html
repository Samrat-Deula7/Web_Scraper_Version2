Test Preparation

IELTS

IELTS is a test that checks how well you use English. For students from non-English-speaking countries, it’s essential. Furthermore, if you plan to study in Australia from Nepal, a good IELTS score helps universities see that you’re ready to learn and communicate confidently in English.

Explore More

PTE

PTE Academic is a trusted computer-based English test for study abroad and immigration. Moreover, if you plan to study in Australia from Nepal, most Australian universities accept PTE scores. The best part is that results are quick-you usually get them within five business days.

Explore More

SAT

The SAT measures the knowledge and skills students gain in school, focusing on critical thinking and problem-solving. Furthermore, if you plan to study in Australia from Nepal, SAT scores can strengthen your application for certain undergraduate programs.
