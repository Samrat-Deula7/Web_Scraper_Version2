    About Shri Siddhivinayak Educational Mahavidhyalaya, Agra

    SSEM, or Shri Siddhivinayak Educational Mahavidhyalaya, Agra is a relatively young university that was established in 2018. Off late, this affiliated college has come up in Raibha, Agra, Uttar Pradesh, and has been gaining reputation in the area. SSEM's areas of specialisation are spread over a small campus of 3 acres and offer 11 courses in 8 programmes for students of 545. It is accredited to the National Council of Teacher Education (NCTE), and the college is noted for its standard in the provision of education. As many as 15 educators work in SSEM faculty; thus, the institution still provides the appropriate student-to-teacher ratio. 

    The colleges have stocked up on contemporary amenities that could make the learning process a little more interesting and advanced. The cornerstone of SSEM’s academic resources is a well-coordinated, computerised library. Equipping this knowledge hub with the latest books and journals in pharmacy and related areas, along with e-resources, is also available here. 

    The library is an important students’ resource that is used in classes and can also be employed in student’s self-education processes. To encourage practical learning, SSEM has equipped high-tech labs for all streams like physical science, chemistry, zoology, botany, etc. These laboratories are well prepared to fulfil standards specified for B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees, so essentially students can get adequate exposure to academic environments as well as industrial exposure. 

    It is also important to make mention of the fact that SSEM provides over 140 academic programmes, courses, and fields of study to interest society and suit every individual’s needs and/or aspirations. The institute offers 11 regular programmes of study at the bachelors and masters levels of education.
