Discover Om Eminent Education

And Research Centre

Om Eminent Education & Research Centre Pvt. Ltd., a top educational service provider in Nepal, was founded in 2009 under Nepal’s company act. Our goal is to support Nepalese students who want to advance their careers through higher education.

Based in Kathmandu, Om Eminent has a skilled team of professionals dedicated to offering quality counseling. We help students choose the right colleges, universities, and programs that suit their needs and abilities. We also assist with visa documentation and guide eligible students in securing placements at international institutions.
