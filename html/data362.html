Welcome to Shaping Minds Group - Education for All (Pvt). Ltd.

  1. Business Services
  2. Shaping Minds Group – Education for All (Pvt). Ltd.

Shaping Minds Group - Education for All (Pvt). Ltd. is a leading educational consultant with more than 15 years of experience on expertise level. We are government registered company based in Kathmandu with a branch office operating in Pokhara as well. The sincere dedication towards future of students and unwavering belief in ethical counseling is the concrete foundation of SMG. We are a team of resolute professionals strongly determined to provide students with best choice for their education and future careers. We cherish our existing profile with pride. Our achievement regarding close associations within innumerable top instituitions and universities during this tenure is remarkable. With as much assurance as much as pride, we state that we have established ourselves as a successful gateway to support your dream to fly across to your dreamland where your aspirations of pursuing your future career awaits. Furthermore, SMG has the best suited test preparation classes available along with excellent grooming. Classes are available in flexible time-period. Highly experienced professionals use different teaching methods for making your learning more effective. Besides, we even facilitate you with mock tests, revision classes and special English classes as per requirements. Thus, we believe a student to be our responsibility where we we mould them into eligible and perfect candidate to pursue their career abroad. Specialties: Experienced professionals, Effective classes with different learning styles, Extra special classes as per requirements, Ethical counseling, and Links with top universities in global range
