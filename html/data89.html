FAQ

What countries can I apply to through Raffles Educare?

At Raffles Educare, we proudly guide students to top destinations like the UK, USA, Australia, Canada and more. If your dream is to study abroad, we’re here to make that journey real.

Do I need IELTS/PTE/Duolingo to apply for a student visa?

It depends on the university you choose, but the good news is that you have options. Whether it’s IELTS, PTE, or Duolingo, we’ll help you choose the best path to meet your university and visa requirements.

Can I apply with a gap in my academic history?

Absolutely! What matters is not just where you’ve been, but where you’re going. We specialize in building strong student profiles, even for students with academic gaps. However, some universities do have specific gap acceptance limits.

How do I choose the right course and university?

Choosing the right path is a big decision but you’re not alone. Our expert counsellors will help match your goals with the best universities and courses worldwide.

How much does it cost to study in the UK/USA/America?

Costs vary, but with the right planning and guidance, it becomes manageable. We’ll walk you through tuition, living expenses, and every detail step by step.

Can Raffles help me with student accommodation?

Yes, we do. From finding safe, affordable housing to settling in comfortably, Raffles makes sure your home away from home feels just right.

How do I book a one-on-one counselling session?

It’s simple, just click ‘Book a Counsellor’ on our website or give us a call. We’ll sit down, listen, and map out a plan tailored just for you.
