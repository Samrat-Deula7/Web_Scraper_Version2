About Us

Boston Educational Services Pvt. Ltd., is located at the heart of capital Kathmandu. The idea of running a fair and sound consultancy occurred to us quiet a long time back but due to the topsy-turvy situations of the country we were unable to materialize the broad concept at that time. Now we are proud to announce that we have already initiated our mission albeit it being much later than anticipated.

We feel very satisfied that the aim of Boston has highly been supported and appreciated by a large number of parents by handing their entire faith over us. At this point we are, by no other reason than the sound success of our various students, confident that the students are willing to stick their names to the Boston Educational Services Pvt. Ltd. And finally we promise the students as well as their most revered parents that the aim of the Boston is to lead the students to their future destinations.
