Click International Education was founded in 2015 with the main goal of offering guidance and support as well as ethical counseling to students who want to pursue further studies in different countries. Our team of certified counselors and committed staff work closely with students, focusing on their goals and qualifications, to provide the best possible guidance for their education and career paths. To help students prepare as needed, we offer various test preparation classes, giving personal attention to each student so they can build confidence for exams and interviews. We have a balanced combination of expert counselors/staff, experienced instructors, spacious classrooms and modern teaching mechanisms to provide top-notch quality service to the students.
