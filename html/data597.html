What We Offer

Our Services

From your first consultation to settling in abroad, Nexsus provides comprehensive support at every step of your study abroad journey with 17+ years of experience.

Career Counseling

Get personalized guidance from our experienced counselors to choose the right course and university based on your goals, budget, and career aspirations.

Test Preparation

Comprehensive IELTS, PTE, and TOEFL preparation with experienced instructors in our modern language labs at Dillibazar, Kathmandu.

Application Support

End-to-end assistance with university applications, documentation, SOP writing, and ensuring your application stands out.

Visa Assistance

Expert visa application guidance with 97% visa success rate. We ensure accurate documentation for a smoother visa process.

Accommodation

Help finding safe and affordable student accommodation, homestays, or shared apartments in your destination country.

Financial Guidance

Scholarship search assistance, education loan guidance, and budget planning to make your study abroad dreams affordable.

Health Cover

Guidance on OSHC (Australia), NHS (UK), and other health insurance requirements to keep you protected abroad.

Pre-Departure Support

Comprehensive orientation sessions covering travel tips, cultural adaptation, and what to expect in your new country.
