About us

Who we are

Way Education is your trusted partner on the journey to studying abroad. With years of experience, we provide comprehensive services; including consulting, travel assistance, and visa support.  Our guiding principles are encapsulated in our “WAY – Warmth, Accompany, Yearning”.

At Way Education, we’re not just a service provider; we want to bring warmth to our students as a way to make them feel pleasant by being the trustful companion who supports them during the journey to make their strong desire come true.

Our services

What we offer

Your study abroad journey deserves more than just guidance—it needs a trusted partner. From finding the perfect university to navigating applications, we’re with you every step of the way, turning your dreams into reality.

Expert
Consultation

A group of dedicated specialists at WAY Education are committed to the well-being of our students. With years of professional experience, our advisory staff helps students with obstacles they encounter before, during, and even after their study abroad experience.

Application
support

Our advisors offer students the most up-to-date and accurate information to ensure they are well-informed throughout the process. In addition, we assist with the distribution and translation of important documents, helping to simplify and streamline the application process even further.

Visa
Support

WAY offers a highly efficient visa application process that includes comprehensive steps and essential information tailored to each type of visa. Additionally, if any issues arise during the application process, our team is fully committed to providing complete assistance and support.
