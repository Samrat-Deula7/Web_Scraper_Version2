Who we are?

 

Open Vision Int’l Educational Consultancy Pvt. Ltd is one of the leading educational consultancy in Nepal, established with in the objective of providing the better counseling to the Nepalese students aspiring to pursue their higher studies in abroad. It also seeks to provide counseling for work & study, research & development in higher education sector and career development. This institute conducts the classes in TOEFL, IELTS, NAT, JLPT, TOP J, J-TEST and GNK with English learning classes.

Open vision is the consultancy founded with the mission of excellence educational consultancy services to promote the standard and quality in diverse needs in education sector of Nepal. We are highly committed and conscious in our duties and responsibilities. We feel proud to be enabled in bridging numerous students with their success and career and at the same time welcome you all seeking for genuine services to grab your life’s destination being a part of open vision.

Our Objectives

Learn More

Provide Accurate Information

We ensure that students receive precise and updated information about institutions and courses, empowering them to make well-informed decisions about their future studies abroad.

Application & Offer Letter Assistance

We streamline the application process and collaborate closely with institutions to secure Offer or Acceptance Letters promptly, making the process seamless for every student.

Consistent Student & Institution Follow-up

We maintain regular communication with both students and institutions, ensuring any queries are addressed quickly and every application is tracked until final confirmation arrives.

Orientation & Culture Awareness

We educate students about the education system, local customs, culture, and environment of their chosen destination, ensuring a smooth adjustment and a successful academic journey abroad.
