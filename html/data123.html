About Studylane International

Who We Are

Know more about our Consultancy


Since its establishment in 2009, Studylane has become a trusted name in international education consultancy. With over a decade of experience, they have built a strong reputation for providing genuine and transparent guidance to students aspiring to study in countries such as Australia, Canada, the UK, the USA, and New Zealand. Their team of certified counselors brings deep expertise, offering personalized support tailored to each student’s unique academic journey.

From simplifying the visa process to helping secure scholarships based on individual profiles, Studylane is committed to delivering a smooth and enriching experience at every step. More than just advisors, they serve as steadfast partners in shaping successful futures. Their mission is rooted in authenticity and reliability, forming the foundation of their unwavering support.

But Studylane’s commitment goes beyond academics. They understand that studying abroad is a life-changing experience—one that blends personal growth, cultural immersion, and academic pursuit. That's why they provide comprehensive support services designed to meet students' academic, emotional, and social needs. From pre-departure orientations to post-arrival adjustment support, they ensure every student’s transition is seamless and rewarding.

Furthermore, Studylane fosters a vibrant and inclusive community through curated events, workshops, and networking opportunities—helping students build meaningful connections and lasting friendships along their journey.

Our Services

Test Preparation

Studylane offers specialized preparation programs for two of the most recognized English language proficiency exams: the IELTS (International English Language Testing System) and the Pearson Test of English (PTE). Their comprehensive courses are crafted to help students achieve their desired scores, which are often necessary for admission to universities and colleges in English-speaking countries.


Their expert instructors provide personalized lessons, practice materials, and strategies to improve skills in listening, reading, writing, and speaking. Through intense preparation and mock exams, students gain confidence and become familiar with the test formats, ensuring they perform their best on exam day. Whether aiming to meet minimum language requirements or striving for higher scores to enhance academic opportunities, Studylane's test preparation courses offer the guidance and support necessary for success.


Visa Application

Navigating the visa application process can be overwhelming, but at Studylane, they provide expert guidance to make it as smooth as possible for Nepalese students. Their visa assistance services ensure transparency, efficiency, and reliability at every step.


Their experienced counselors help students throughout the entire visa application process, from gathering required documentation to preparing for embassy or consulate interviews. They provide updated information on visa requirements and procedures to ensure students are well-prepared.

With Studylane's guidance, students can confidently focus on their studies while they take care of their visa application process, helping them pursue their academic goals abroad.


Education Counseling

Studylane's certified counselors, with over a decade of experience, provide tailored guidance for Nepalese students aspiring to study abroad. Their services focus on understanding each student’s unique needs and aspirations, helping them make informed decisions about their educational future.


Their counselors assist with course selection, choosing the right institution and country based on academic background, career goals, and personal preferences. They provide in-depth information on various study destinations, academic programs, admission requirements, and scholarships, empowering students to make confident decisions.


They prioritize open communication, transparency, and reliability throughout the counseling process, ensuring students have the support they need from the initial consultation through to application submission. Studylane’s education counseling helps students begin their study abroad journey with clarity and confidence.


Scholarship Guidance
At Studylane, they recognize that funding an education abroad can be challenging. That’s why they offer expert scholarship guidance to help Nepalese students secure financial aid.


With a successful track record of helping students obtain scholarships covering 10% to 100% of tuition fees, they are dedicated to maximizing scholarship opportunities tailored to each student's profile and achievements.


Their experienced counselors help identify scholarships offered by universities, government agencies, private organizations, and more. They provide assistance with application processes, eligibility criteria, required documentation, and deadlines, ensuring students submit competitive applications. With Studylane's scholarship guidance, students can pursue their academic goals without financial concerns.


Documentation Assistance


They understand that handling documentation for studying abroad can be overwhelming, so Studylane provides complete support to help Nepalese students navigate the paperwork efficiently.


Their experienced team helps students compile and organize essential documents, including application essays, academic transcripts, and visa forms. They ensure all documents are accurate and submitted according to the specific requirements, helping students avoid delays and complications.


With Studylane's documentation services, students can rest assured that their paperwork is in good hands, allowing them to focus on preparing for their study abroad journey with confidence.


Pre-Departure to Post-Departure Services


Studylane’s support extends beyond the application process, offering comprehensive pre-departure and post-departure services to ensure students are fully prepared for every aspect of their study abroad experience.


Pre-Departure Services:

  • Visa Application Assistance: Their counselors provide personalized support to ensure all documents are in order and help with embassy interviews.

  • Travel Arrangements: They assist with booking flights and arranging transportation to ensure smooth travel logistics.

  • Accommodation Assistance: Studylane helps students find appropriate housing options, including on-campus housing, homestays, or private rentals.

  • Orientation Sessions: They conduct orientation to prepare students for life abroad, covering cultural norms, academic expectations, and practical tips.

Post-Departure Services:

  • Academic Advising: Their team offers ongoing guidance on course selection, academic resources, and study strategies.

  • Cultural Adjustment Assistance: They support students as they navigate cultural differences and adjust to life in a new country.

  • Alumni Networking: Studylane connects current students with alumni, offering networking, mentorship, and support opportunities.


Seminars and Workshops
Studylane organizes regular seminars and workshops for Nepalese students aspiring to study abroad. These events cover a wide range of topics, such as admissions processes, academic requirements, career opportunities, and cultural adaptation.


Admission Weeks
They also host special events, such as Study Abroad Admission Weeks, where students can learn about various study destinations and institutions. During these events, university and college representatives provide valuable information about their programs and admissions procedures.


One-to-One Counseling
Alongside group seminars and workshops, Studylane offers one-on-one counseling sessions tailored to each student’s needs. These sessions provide personalized guidance, and representatives from partner institutions may also participate, giving students direct access to valuable insights.


Student Health Insurance
Studylane International provides comprehensive Student Health Insurance to safeguard the well-being of students studying abroad. Their policies cover medical expenses, including doctor visits, hospital stays, prescription drugs, and emergency medical evacuation. Mental health services and pre-existing conditions are also covered, ensuring students have access to essential healthcare throughout their academic journey.

This assistance offers peace of mind for students and their families, allowing them to focus on their studies without worrying about healthcare expenses.
