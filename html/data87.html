HOW WE HELP

Our Services

Pre-Departure Guidance

Empowering successful student visa holders by offering assistance in organizing essential travel and accommodation arrangements. Additionally, providing comprehensive pre-departure orientation to counsel and ensure a seamless settlement abroad. Our support aims to enhance the overall experience for international students.

Visa Support

Assisting students in preparing precise visa documentation in accordance with the latest requirements and checklists from the Embassy/Consulate/Visa Sections of Australia, USA, UK, Canada, and New Zealand. Our guidance ensures a smooth and compliant visa application process for international education.

Application Assistance

Facilitating the application process for students, ensuring adherence to institutions' guidelines, and assisting in securing admission letters. Our streamlined approach enhances the efficiency of the application process for a seamless experience.

Course Selection

Helping students select the ideal course and degree level based on their academic, professional background, and future goals. Our guidance ensures alignment with their plans for optimal academic and career success.

Abroad Studies

Through the collaboration of dedicated full-time education counselors and our partner's International Admissions Office, we deliver genuine and reliable information to students aspiring to pursue education abroad. Our committed professionals work seamlessly to assist those seeking to study overseas.

Test Preparation

Annually, KIEC facilitates more than 600 students in their pursuit of success in IELTS, TOEFL, PTE, SAT, and GRE/GMAT exams. Our rigorous and outcome-driven test preparation courses, led by proficient, certified, and seasoned academic teams, ensure comprehensive readiness for these crucial exams.

Who we are

Welcome to Kathmandu International Education Center (KIEC)

Since 2011, Your Trusted Partner in Educational Excellence!

KIEC was founded with a visionary purpose: to empower Nepalese students to pursue higher education abroad. What began as a dynamic initiative by a group of experienced educators and counselors has evolved into a premier educational consultancy committed to guiding students toward global academic opportunities.

Our journey offers students a transformative experience, fostering cultural immersion, academic enrichment, and personal growth.
