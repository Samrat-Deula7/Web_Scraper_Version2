Our Vision

Interpacific Educational Foundation Nepal Pvt. Ltd (IEFN), is committed to explore the qualities of Nepalese students and finding better opportunities for them throughout the world. Our belief is on qualitative education which sharpens the horizon of their academic ability in diverse field. We are always and ever committed to consult with students and provide them guidance for the better opportunities those who are competitive, enthusiastic and able to explore and demonstrate the bright future of country as well by supporting students to acquire international level of education to build strength so that they can contribute in the nation building.
