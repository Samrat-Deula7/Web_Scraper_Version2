Dr.S. SANTHANAKRISHNAN MD

Founder and Chairman​

ROYALGLO NEXT ACADEMY, CHENNAI

In 2008, Dr. S. Santhanakrishnan, MD, a general practitioner who had finished his medical studies in Russia and passed the MCI FMGE Screening exam, founded RoyalGlo Next Academy Pvt Ltd. He began his career as an Indian medical registered practitioner and founded an direct education academy for study MBBS in Georgia and MD programs with the goal of providing Indian students who wanted to pursue these programs overseas with high-quality medical instruction.

Georgia,China, Armenia, Egypt and the United States are among the countries where RoyalGlo Next, an MBBS abroad, offers services with high standards of admission (study MBBS/MD). Professional doctors also tutor students for the USMLE, FMGE, and NEET UG and PG exams at our academy.

In 2009, we began our MBBS Direct Admission Office in a modest way and were able to enroll 20 students for the study.Indian students are sent to a medical college in MBBS Abroad in order to receive a higher caliber of medical education. In 2010, we sent 1092 students to study MBBS/MD at medical colleges abroad. Through our organization, over 1500 doctors earned their medical degrees (MBBS/MD) overseas education. In Georgia, 1200 students have been admitted to study medicine through the MBBS program.

The success of the school is greatly attributed to the 55 knowledgeable faculty members who are essential to RoyalGlo Next International Academy Pvt Ltd. Their unselfish service and dedication to the students are crucial.

I WELCOME THE STUDENTS AND PARENTS

Digital and distance learning programs, as well as smart classrooms, are how we deliver our services. Our students consistently score highly and perform exceptionally well on a variety of medical entrance exams, including the MCI, FMGE Screening Test, NEET, USMLE, AIIMS, and JIPMER.
