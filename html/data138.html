Budhanilkantha international academy

Guiding dreams since 2002, Study in Japan, USA, Australia, Europe, UK, Canada & more | Visa, Documentation & Scholarships – Your trusted study abroad partner.

Founded in 2002, Budhanilakantha International Academy has been a trusted name in study-abroad consultancy for over two decades. We are dedicated to guiding students toward achieving their dreams of quality education in world-class institutions. Our mission is simple: to connect your potential with global opportunities. We provide personalized counseling, complete documentation support, and pre-de

parture guidance so you can focus on building your future with confidence. Our Study Destinations
We assist students in pursuing education in the world’s most sought-after academic hubs:

Japan – Known for its high-quality education, cutting-edge technology, and cultural richness, Japan offers diverse opportunities, especially in engineering, IT, and research fields. USA – Home to top-ranked universities and limitless academic programs, the USA is the ideal destination for innovative and career-focused education. Australia – With globally recognized degrees and a safe, welcoming environment, Australia is perfect for students seeking quality and work opportunities. Europe – Offering a blend of academic excellence and cultural diversity, Europe is home to affordable or even tuition-free education in many countries. New Zealand – Known for its friendly communities and practical learning approach, New Zealand is great for students who value work-life balance alongside studies. Germany – Famous for its tuition-free or low-cost education, especially in engineering, science, and research programs. France – A hub for fashion, arts, business, and innovation, France provides high-quality education with rich cultural experiences. Korea – Popular for modern education, technology, and vibrant student life, Korea offers scholarships and programs taught in English. UK – Home to world-class universities with strong reputations in almost every field, the UK is ideal for short yet impactful academic programs. Canada – Known for its top-tier education, multicultural environment, and excellent post-study work opportunities. Our Services
At Budhanilakantha International Academy, we provide complete support from your first inquiry to your arrival abroad:

Visa Processing – Expert assistance to ensure smooth and successful visa approval. Documentation – Guidance on preparing all necessary documents for both admission and visa application. Pre-Departure Information – Cultural orientation, travel tips, and survival guidance to help you adjust abroad. Scholarship Assistance – Helping eligible students apply for partial or full scholarships offered by universities and governments.
