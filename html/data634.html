Studying in Japan

Requirements for Studying in Japan

Dreaming of studying abroad? 🌏✈️

We make your dream a reality! Whether you want to explore new cultures, gain international exposure, or pursue world-class education, we guide you every step of the way.

Our Services:

  • University Selection: Find the perfect course and university that fits your goals.

  • Application Support: Assistance with forms, documents, and submissions.

  • Visa Guidance: Smooth visa process with expert support.

  • Pre-Departure Help: Travel tips, accommodation, and settling abroad

Application Process

  1. Consultation & Course Selection – Meet our counselors to choose the right program.
  2. Japanese Language Preparation – Enroll in language classes.
  3. University/College Application – Submit required documents.
  4. Financial Documentation – Prepare bank statements and sponsorship letters.
  5. Visa Application & Interview – Submit visa documents and attend the interview.
  6. Pre-Departure Orientation – Get ready for Japan!

Required Documents

  1. Academic Transcripts & Certificates (SLC/SEE, +2, Bachelor’s, etc.).
  2. Japanese Language Certificate (NAT/JLPT/TOPJ Certificate).
  3. Valid Passport (minimum 1-year validity).
  4. Visa Application Form (filled and signed).
  5. Statement of Purpose (SOP) – Explaining study plans and future goals.
  6. Letter of Guarantee from financial sponsor.
  7. Police Clearance Certificate.
  8. Medical Report (Health Checkup Certificate).

Financial Requirements

  1. Bank balance of at least 20-25 lakhs NPR to show financial stability.
  2. Sponsorship letter (if applicable) from parents or guardians.
  3. Occupation verification of  your sponsor 

Japanese Language Proficiency

  1. N5 level Japanese language proficiency is mandatory (N4/N3 preferred for university admission).
  2. Language courses and certification from recognized institutions.

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum Grades: +2 Pass ✅

  • Required Subjects: Depends on your chosen course – we guide you!

  • Language Proficiency: JAPANESE /ENGLISH /Korean, if required.

  • Additional Certificates: Any extra qualifications for your program.

No worries! We make sure you meet all requirements and help you prepare a strong application for a bright future.
