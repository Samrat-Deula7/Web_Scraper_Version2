    Frequently Asked Questions

    Crack the success to studying abroad with the best consultancy services at CSC Global!

    Is a study gap acceptable in the UK?

    Yes, the UK accepts study gaps, provided you can demonstrate a valid reason for the break.

    How much does it cost to be a student in Canada?

    Canada is known for offering affordable education compared to many other countries. Tuition fees typically range from C$20,000 to C$30,000 per year, depending on the program and university.

    How long can I stay in Australia after completing my studies?

    After completing your studies in Australia, you may be eligible for a Temporary Graduate Visa (subclass 485), which allows you to stay in Australia and work for up to 2 to 4 years, depending on your qualification level and field of study.

    Is New Zealand a good destination for international students?

    Yes, New Zealand is known for its high-quality education system, safe environment, and welcoming culture.

    What are the post-study work opportunities in the USA?

    After graduation, international students can apply for Optional Practical Training (OPT), which allows them to work in the USA for up to 12 months (or up to 36 months for STEM graduates). OPT provides a great opportunity to gain work experience in your field of study.
