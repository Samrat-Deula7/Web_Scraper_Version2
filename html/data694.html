Build Trust with the Verified Employer Badge

Gain higher visibility, improve applicant confidence, and present your organization as a credible, trusted employer on Merojob.

Step 01

Provide Company Details

Provide your basic organization details including company name, PAN/VAT, contact details, and address.

Step 02

Upload Required Documents

Submit verification documents such as company registration, PAN/VAT certificate, or other official papers.

Step 03

Review & Approval

Our team reviews your submission and activates your Verified Employer Badge once approved.

What Verification Means ?

Verification confirms that your organization is legitimate by reviewing your company details and submitted documents.

Once verified, your profile and job posts display a Verified Badge, helping jobseekers instantly recognize trusted employers.

Why Getting Verified Matters

01
Earn Instant Trust

Verified companies experience higher credibility & better applicant engagement.

02
Attract Better Candidates

Authenticity encourages more qualified and committed jobseekers to apply.

03
Strengthen Your Brand

Showcase your company as an authentic, transparent organisation that values credibility.
