Future Way Educational Consultancy Pvt. Ltd

Welcome to Futureway Educational Consultancy

Future Way Educational Consultancy is the most trusted and reputed educational consultancy in Nepal, established in 2016 A.D to assist Nepalese students to achieve their study overseas dreams in Australia, Canada, USA, Europe, New Zealand and the UK.

Future Way Educational Consultancy Nepal is an overseas education consultancy led and managed by dedicated consultants with extensive know-how on placement and career counseling. Services currently offered by our team include counseling the prospective students, identifying colleges and universities abroad, assured admissions, comprehensive preparation for the visa interview, and also pre- departure orientation. The core activity lies in providing accurate and realistic counseling to the students willing to pursue further education abroad. Future Way Educational Consultancy is also a training institute that offers preparation courses for international tests like IELTS, PTE, TOEFL & SAT. We are a team of professionals who believe in growth, harmony and integrity. We take an opportunity providing personalized consultancy to students at all stages of education, from school to university level. Our advisors are experienced. Our tutors are among the best in their specializations and deliver result-oriented lectures by generating appropriate environment.

Future Way educational consultancy consists of a highly professional team of certified counselors and experienced staffs, devoted to providing effective career counseling and documentation services to students willing to study in various country such as Australia, USA, Canada, and Europe etc.
