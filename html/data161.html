About Supreme Educational Centre Pvt Ltd:
Supreme Educational Centre Pvt. Ltd., established in 2001, is a prominent educational consultancy based in Kathmandu, Nepal. Located at Kumaripaty, Lalitpur, Nepal( Next to Muktinath Development Bank), the center operates from 7:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and on Sunday. As a leading educational counseling center, Supreme Educational Centre assists students in selecting appropriate study-abroad destinations to enhance their skills and career prospects in today's global market. Their services include admission guidance, career counseling, university selection, document management, interview preparation, and travel and accommodation arrangements. They cater to countries such as Australia, Europe, Japan, and the USA. The center's mission emphasizes growth with a focus on excellence, honesty, integrity, and up-to-date knowledge. They aim to build strong relationships with partner institutions and provide tailored solutions for students aspiring to study abroad. Their core values include honesty, commitment, transparency, and value addition. Supreme Educational Centre is a registered member of the Educational Consultancy Association of Nepal (ECAN), reflecting their commitment to maintaining industry standards and best practices. Frequently Asked Question (FAQs)

What services does Supreme Educational Centre offer?:
Supreme Educational Centre provides comprehensive services to students aspiring to study abroad. These services include educational counseling, university selection, admission assistance, visa processing, and language preparation classes such as IELTS, PTE, and Japanese language courses.
Which countries can Supreme Educational Centre assist with for study abroad?:
The consultancy assists students in pursuing higher education in various countries, including Australia, the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, Japan, and several European nations.
How can I contact Supreme Educational Centre?:
You can reach Supreme Educational Centre by visiting their office at Kumaripaty, Lalitpur, Nepal( Next to Muktinath Development Bank).
Is Supreme Educational Centre a registered member of any educational associations?:

What is the mission and vision of Supreme Educational Centre?:
The mission of Supreme Educational Centre is to grow their business with an emphasis on excellence, honesty, integrity, and updated knowledge.
