    FAQ'sFrequently Asked Questions

    How to process Japan Student Visa ?

    Want to study in Japan?

    Here is the Complete Student Visa Application Process in easy language 

     Student Visa Process:

    • Academic documents preparation (SLC / +2 / Bachelor)

    • Starting Japanese language classes (JLPT / NAT / JLCT)

    • Choosing a Japanese language school or college

    • Applying to the school and getting admission

    • Applying for a Certificate of Eligibility (COE)

    • Applying for a Visa at the Japanese Embassy after receiving the COE

    • Preparing for travel after visa approval 

    While studying in Japan, you also get **part-time work opportunities**, which helps you manage your study and living.

    How to get a working visa in Japan with a bachelor's degree ?

    If you have a Bachelor's Degree, you have the opportunity to build a good career in Japan through a Professional Working Visa.

    Process at a glance:

    • Preparation of Bachelor level academic documents

    • Job search according to the relevant field

    • Learning Japanese language

    • Applying to a Japanese company

    • Receiving a Job Offer from the company

    • Applying for a Certificate of Eligibility (COE)

    • Applying for a Visa from the Japanese Embassy

    • Leaving for Japan with a work permit

    Opportunities are available in various professional fields including Engineering, IT, Hospitality, Management.

    How to Apply for SSW Visa?

    If you want to go to Japan with a Working Visa, it is very important to understand the SSW (Specified Skilled Worker) process.

     SSW Application Process:

    1. Passport and Educational Certificate Preparation
    2. Learn Japanese (JLPT N4 or JFT Basic is mandatory)
    3.  Pass Skill Evaluation Test according to the employment field
    4. Apply to a Japanese Company and get a Job Offer
    5. Apply for COE (Certificate of Eligibility) from the Company
    6. Apply for Visa at the Japanese Embassy
    7. Japan Departure with Work Permit
