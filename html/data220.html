Our Strength

Darwin Education Foundation

Darwin Education Foundation is centrally located inside Kathmandu City at Putalisadak Kathmandu. This education consultancy has excellent networks with Universities abroad in the USA, Denmark, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Norway, Germany, Japan, and Cyprus and provides a solid backbone with years of experience in exams and coaching, career counseling, University selection, recommendation, financial assistance, travel assistance, scholarship guidance and VISA documentation.

Darwin offers attractive discount packages for students who want to take part in IELTS and English Language classes, Public speaking, and professional writing courses.  Besides Darwin Education Consultancy it has a sister Company called Darwin Air Hostess Training Academy Pvt. Ltd. to provide trained aviation manpower to aviation Companies for service.

With the help of this Consultancy, our students can study programs such as BBA, MBA, BHM, HM, travel, and tourism in foreign Universities and can also have part-time job opportunities through work permits and VISA success, limited tuition fees in foreign Countries following footsteps and invaluable advice from the team of expert consultants of Darwin Education Foundation. They also pre-inform about coaching classes resume dates on their website and impart any vital information required in the process of abroad education specifically for aspiring students in overseas education.
