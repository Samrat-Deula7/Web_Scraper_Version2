About Skills to Earn

Skills to Earn is a training and certification organization that offers wide range of international standard customised training and certification. It also helps institutions in upgrading their education/training standards.

The courses offered here are joint certification program by Skills to Earn and Training Qualifications UK (TQUK). The curriculum, syllabus, delivery system and evaluation system is developed to match with local requirement and as per the international standard.
