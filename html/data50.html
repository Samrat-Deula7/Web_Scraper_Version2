Welcome to Delta Educare

Delta Educare Nepal Pvt Ltd, trading as Delta Educare, is an overseas education consultancy led and managed by dedicated consultants with extensive know-how on placement and career counseling. Services currently offered by our team include counseling the prospective students, identifying colleges and universities abroad, assured admissions, comprehensive preparation for the visa interview, and also pre-departure orientation. The core activity lies in providing accurate and realistic counselling to the students willing to pursue further education abroad. Our placement wing specializes in English speaking countries like USA, UK, Ireland, Australia, New Zealand & Canada.

Looking for services par excellence? Choose Delta Educare.

Delta Educare, a leading name in overseas education market in Pokhara, prides itself on the professional integrity it has developed and maintained since its inception. We are locally owned, nationally recognized and internationally accredited company. Trust-building at Delta has been a matter of survival, pursued with relentless and cool-headed determination by an able and cohesive team, and students’ satisfaction has been at the heart of this.

Students seeking our services often choose us because they see that we have few things quite different to others in the industry, and that’s what makes us stand out prominently.

Comprehensive Counseling

Our counseling wing is known for being generous in giving in-depth information to our students and parents. Our advice is elaborate and realistic and we enjoy responding to every minor query we get from students before they decide to apply abroad. We are never too tired to talk to them.

First Hand Information

Seeing is believing! We believe in it because we have seen it. Our counselors have themselves studied and stayed abroad for many years, and they regularly visit foreign universities and colleges. Therefore you can expect to get first hand information about what it really feels like to study abroad, both in terms of opportunities and challenges you may have as an international student abroad.

Accreditations

Accreditations are our assets. Our expertise in the industry can be reflected through a number of endorsements we possess. Our team includes QEAC (Qualified Education Agent Counselor), ITAC (ICEF Trained Agent Counselor), ENZ Trained staffs, and British Council & ETS certified instructors. In addition, we, a proud member of ECAN (Education Consultancies’ Association of Nepal), are approved by the Ministry of Education (MOE), Nepal Government, to deliver our services. So, you can count on us.

Above-Average Test Results

We have had an outstanding track record in IELTS, PTE & TOEFL results. It is because we apply a unique and hands on learning approach in test preparation. Our academic supports and resources are unlimited. Our lesson plans for the delivery of instructions are most up to date and comprehensive.

High Visa Success

When it comes to visa success rates, we simply rock! Among many, one important reason for it is the scrupulous documentation and effective interview preparation we provide to our students. As our students succeed in getting visas through us, we rely on their referrals and recommendations, and that’s what has helped us come this far.
