    Our Services

    Kokorozashi Japanese Culture & Research Center aims to provide a platform for exchange of ideas and knowledge between Japan and Nepal. To achieve these goals, the center conducts various cultural activities and events, such as language classes, workshops on traditional arts and crafts, and exhibitions on Japanese culture and history.
