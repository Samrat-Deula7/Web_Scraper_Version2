Four Step Way To Japan

Setp 1

Get Free Consultation & Profile Check

  • Talk to our team about your goal (study or work in Japan)

  • We check if you're eligible for a suitable visa (SSW, student, etc.)

  • We guide you on what training or documents you will need

Step 2

Start Language & SSW Training

  • Join our Japanese language classes (JFT or JLPT)

  • Take SSW training if you're applying for a SSW visa

  • Prepare for interviews and pass required tests

Step 3

Documentation & Apply for COE

  • We help you collect and prepare required documents

  • Submit documents to apply for the Certificate of Eligibility (COE)

  • Wait for approval from the Japan Immigration Office

Step 4

Get Visa & Fly to Japan

  • After COE is approved, we apply for your visa

  • Attend the visa interview (if required)

  • Once approved, book your flight and get ready for Japan!
