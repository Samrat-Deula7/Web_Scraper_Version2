Welcome to Prime Education Information Center


Established in 2002, Prime Education Information Center is an education consultancy in Nepal, and is committed and dedicated to assist for overall guidance and assistance to the students aspiring to pursue further and higher education abroad. As located at one of the prime location in Kathmandu, it is easily accessible from any corner of the city. We have a team of well experienced professionals for counselling on courses and universities, admission assistance, visa guidance and pre departure orientation. Our commitment and dedication for genuine and quality services have enabled us in being a best education consultancy in Nepal.
