About Prime Education

Established in 2002, Prime Education Information Center is an education consultancy, committed and dedicated to assist for overall guidance and assistance to the students aspiring to pursue further and higher education abroad. As it is located at one of the prime location, it is easily accessible from any corner in the city. We have a team of certified counsellors and well experienced professionals for admission counselling, documentation, assistance, visa guidance with mock interviews.

With a team of well qualified, experienced and dynamic professionals, Prime Education welcomes prospective students for admission information, documentation assistance, and visa application guidance with mock interviews. We represent a well number of esteemed institutions from the USA, Australia, Canada, New Zealand, UK and Thailand.

Diligent and helpful team, experienced counselors, personal attention, factual information, honest suggestion, commitment and accountability are our assets which enabled us to win the hearts of our clients. And, we are proud for being a trustworthy name in the field of educational consulting services in Nepal.

Besides, we also conduct coaching classes for TOEFL, IELTS and SAT by well qualified and experienced instructors with updated and effective learning materials. Our well equipped computer lab is the online and offline practicing place for the above tests. We have very encouraging test scores achieved by the past students.

Prime Education Information Center Pvt. is a member of Educational Consultants Association of Nepal (ECAN) with its membership no. 047/2006/07. This company has been the reliable and meticulous agent of many Universities and Colleges, especially of U.S.A, Canada, U.K., Australia and New Zealand.

Besides, it has been working with some other institutions of Germany and Cyprus as well. Prime Education always run behind deeds not behind words, so we work and help the students diligently in such way that they will be eligible to hold the visa of the country they want for and they have their dreams true to have the academic degrees of foreign countries. Our offices are located at different areas, which are fully facilitated with computing resources (Free Internet, well furnishing lab, sound environment); seminar room with necessary equipments (In focus Projector, TV, Video and multimedia); Library and Class rooms.
