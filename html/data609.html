Who we are ?

A Team of passionate, experienced and well-informed professionals; with in-depth knowledge about all the aspects of abroad study.

What we do ?

We provide unbiased proven, attainable advice specialized to each student’s requirements. Whether it is budget, aspirations, grades or qualifications, we  help to achieve our student’s desire goals.

What makes us different ?

Our interest is that of the student. We help to create plan and pathway to achieve whatever goals they may have. With C.B. International each student has the best resources at their fingertips.

Why Chhoose us ?

ADMISSION COUNSELLING

Our experienced counselling team guides students through every step of the admission process with clear and personalized support. We provide detailed information about universities, entry requirements, and application procedures to ensure accuracy and confidence. With our expert advice, students can make well-informed decisions and secure admission to their desired institutions.

CAREER COUNSELLING

Our career counselling service helps students discover their strengths, explore career pathways, and make informed decisions about their future. We provide personalized guidance tailored to individual goals, aligning academic choices with career aspirations. With expert support, students gain clarity and confidence to pursue the right study and career opportunities.

DOCUMENTATION

We provide comprehensive documentation guidance to help students prepare the required papers for visa approval across various countries. Our experts ensure accuracy and compliance with all regulations, minimizing the chances of errors or delays. With our support, students can confidently complete their applications and move forward in their study abroad journey.

VISA COUNSELING

Our visa counselling service supports students in understanding and navigating complex visa procedures with ease. We provide step-by-step guidance on requirements, documentation, and interview preparation to maximize approval chances. With our expert assistance, students can secure their visas smoothly and focus on their study abroad journey.

 
INTERVIEW & TEST PREPARATION

We offer tailored interview and test preparation to help students present their best selves during admission and visa processes. Our training covers communication skills, confidence building, and common test formats to ensure strong performance. With expert coaching, students gain the skills and readiness needed to succeed in competitive assessments.

ACCOMMODATION

We assist students in finding safe and comfortable accommodation options both on-campus and off-campus as per their needs. Our team ensures housing arrangements that suit individual preferences, budget, and lifestyle. With our support, students can enjoy a smooth transition and a secure living environment while studying abroad.

STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICE

We provide a wide range of student support services designed to make studying abroad stress-free and rewarding. From airport pickup and drop to pre-departure orientation, we ensure students feel prepared and comfortable in their new environment. Our support also includes locality guidance and legal advice, helping students settle smoothly and focus on their studies.
