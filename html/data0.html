Mates International Education and Visa Services

About Mates International Education and Visa Services:

Mate’s Education Pvt. Ltd is one of the pioneer education consultancies of Nepal that helps Nepali students obtain “Admission & Student Visa” to go to different countries, mainly Australia, Canada, UK, and New Zealand. What makes us unique is our ‘equal support’ to those returning to settle back in Nepal by grabbing the new career opportunities or unique business startup ideas. Hence, we are here to help you while going abroad to Learn to Achieve your dream, Earn the Recognition of your successful dignity and even if you wish to Return to Reflect your education, experience, and expertise in Nepal. Abroad Study Plan is not like reading one book or watching one movie or visiting your favorite place for leisure activities. More specifically, it’s not a matter of short span of time.

Instead, it’s a matter of your whole life which is based on your one particular ‘action’ that decides ‘what kind of life’ you will live ahead as consequences. In short, Abroad Study Plan is the “The Transition Phase” of your life which is based on your today’s action and that action is – Choosing the right consultancy.

There are thousands of education consultancies in the market and most of them were, perhaps, born yesterday. Nothing wrong with that, but if your whole Life is going to depend on that Consultancy and their Counselors, you should always choose the one that has a History, the Experience, The Team and the one that can ‘Assure’ your future in abroad or even when you return to Nepal. Like we said, we are here to assist you go and here to assist you return – as your Guide & as your Mate. But we are here in the whole journey of your lifetime.
