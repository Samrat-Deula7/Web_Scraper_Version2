WELCOME TO MIDORI INTERNATIONAL

Midori International Language Institute was established in 2014 located at Putalisadak, Kathmandu with the vision to assist Nepalese students to study in Japan. Midori International has been providing professional consultation service in the areas of educational Consultancy. Midori International has grown rapidly over the years and has provided its services to multiple numbers of students since its establishment. Our professionalism and dedication towards students to make their career can be seen in our services and consistent support. Midori International Language Institute has peen providing three types of courses on the students need and purpose: NAT, JLPT, and TOP-J. This courses is specially designed for those students who want to go and study in Japan Through this institution. We believe in qualitative education, therefore we recognize the students queries first and always make sure to give the best guidance to make the students career better.
