About Us

Humboldt Educational Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2004 at the core of Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal. We specialized in offering best study options, study abroad counseling, documentation assistance, visa guidance, admission, cultural exchange programs, educational research activities and post landing services. We help to bring overseas education within the reach of the Nepalese students through our extensive experience of two decades. We assist students to make the right choice for pursuing the programs of their choices abroad.

Humboldt is the member of International Education Representatives' Initiative of Nepal (IERIN), Nepal Australia Chamber of Commerce and Industries, Nepal Chamber of Commerce. Our counselors are certified by the Ministry of Education of Nepal, Training Institute for technical Instruction (TITI) and trained by the British Council, Nepal.
