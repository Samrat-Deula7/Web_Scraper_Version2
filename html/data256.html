Our Story

Aussie International Education Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2015 AD with a clear purpose to address a growing need in Nepal: the need for an education consultancy that prioritizes student welfare above all else. Located at Bagbazar-28, Kathmandu, Nepal, we have grown into a trusted name for students and families seeking guidance on international education opportunities.
Over the years, we have witnessed firsthand the transformative power of international education. We have seen students from across Nepal go on to build extraordinary careers in medicine, engineering, business, law, and the arts and we have been privileged to play a role in those journeys.
What sets us apart is not just our knowledge of international education systems, but our genuine investment in each student’s success. We understand the weight of the decisions students and families make when choosing to study abroad. We understand the financial sacrifice, the emotional courage, and the long-term commitment it requires.

Aussie International Education Pvt. Ltd. is a premier Nepal-based education consultancy specializing exclusively in connecting qualified Nepalese students with leading universities across the United Kingdom. Founded on the principles of integrity, excellence, and student-first service, we have established ourselves as one of Nepal’s most trusted and professionally accredited UK-focused recruitment partners.
Our mission is to transform the academic ambitions of deserving Nepalese students into successful enrolments at reputable UK institutions while giving every university partner a consultancy, they can trust to represent their programs with accuracy and professionalism.
We carefully match students to the right UK courses based on their academic profile, eligibility, and career goals. We prepare high-quality applications personal statements, CVs, and supporting documents and provide end-to-end support from initial consultation through to CAS processing, visa guidance, and pre-departure preparation. Honest, compliant, and transparent communication with both students and partner institutions is maintained at every stage.

Our Value

Our values define how we operate as a recruitment partner, shaping every
institutional relationship, every student referral, and every commitment we make to
the UK universities we represent.

Integrity

We represent your institution honestly and accurately at all times. Students referred by us arrive with realistic expectations of your programs, fees, and campus environment protecting your reputation and reducing early withdrawal risk.

Compliance

We operate in full alignment with UKVI regulations, British Council agent standards, and your institution's own recruitment policies. Ethical, regulation-first recruitment is not a preference it is our standard practice.

Excellence

We hold ourselves to the highest professional standards in application quality, documentation accuracy, and student preparation ensuring your admissions team receives referrals that are complete, credible, and worth considering.

Accountability

When we make a commitment to a partner university, we honor it. Deadlines, intake targets, documentation requirements, and communication standards are taken seriously and delivered consistently.

Quality Over Volume

We do not priorities application numbers over student suitability. Every referral is screened against your entry criteria because your completion rates and program reputation matter to us as much as they do to you.

Long-Term Partnership

We view our university relationships as enduring institutional partnerships, not transactional arrangements. Our goal is to grow alongside you delivering consistent, reliable student pipelines intake after intake.

ICEF Recognized - International Consultants for Education and Fairs

Aussie International Education Pvt. Ltd. is proudly recognized by ICEF the International Consultants for Education and Fairs one of the most respected and authoritative bodies in the global international education industry.

ICEF recognition is not easily obtained. To be ICEF recognized means that our operations, our processes, and our standards have been assessed and validated against rigorous international benchmarks giving our students, their families, and our university partners the assurance that they are working with a consultancy they can fully trust.

For our partner universities, our ICEF recognition serves as a quality guarantee confirming that every student referred through Aussie International Education has been counseled ethically, prepared thoroughly, and guided with full transparency. For our students, it means peace of mind knowing that the guidance they receive is held to the highest global standards of professional practice.

Being part of the ICEF network also connects us to a global community of education professionals, providing our team with access to the latest industry insights, best practices, and international developments all of which directly benefit the students and institutions we serve.

Aussie International Education Pvt. Ltd. is proudly partnered with IDP | IELTS the
International Development Program one of the most globally recognized and
authoritative names in international education and English language assessment.
IDP’s standing in the international education industry is unparalleled. Established
in 1969 by Australian universities, IDP has grown from its origins as a
development assistance program into a full-service global education organization,
and today stands as one of the three co-owners of the world’s most widely accepted
English language test. IELTS the International English Language Testing System is
jointly owned by IDP Education, the British Council, and Cambridge Assessment
English, representing a tri-partnership of the most respected education institutions
on the planet. That ownership is not a commercial arrangement alone it is a
statement of integrity, rigor, and global trust.
Being an authorized IDP | IELTS partner is not a designation available to every
education consultancy. It signifies that our operations meet the standards of an
organization whose test results are accepted by over 10,000 organizations
worldwide, and whose reputation for fairness, security, and reliability underpins
the study, migration, and career aspirations of millions of students globally. For our
students, this partnership means direct access to official IELTS test registration,
authoritative preparation guidance, and counselling that is fully aligned with the
standards of one of the world’s most trusted education bodies.

Student Eligibility Assessment

Before any application is submitted to your institution, we conduct a thorough review of each student's academic profile, English language proficiency, and financial capacity against your published entry requirements. Only students who meet your criteria proceed.

Course & University Matching

We match students to UK programs based on verified academic history and eligibility not on what a student simply wants. Your entry standards are the benchmark, not the student's preference.

Application Preparation:

Your admissions team receives complete, accurately prepared application packages personal statements, academic transcripts, CVs, and all supporting documentation ready for assessment without the need for follow-up or chasing.

Visa Guidance & UKVI Compliance

We manage the Student Route visa process, CAS acceptance, and financial evidence preparation in full compliance with current UKVI requirements reducing the risk of incomplete or non-compliant submissions reaching your international office.

Pre-Departure Preparation

Students arrive on your campus having been fully briefed on academic expectations, UK student life, accommodation, registration, and their responsibilities as international students minimizing early-stage queries to your student services team.

Post-Enrolment Liaison

Our relationship with students does not end at enrolment. We maintain ongoing contact throughout their studies providing a reliable local point of support that complements your institution's own student welfare provision and helps protect your retention rates..

Our Expertise

Our Expertise: The UK Specialists

The United Kingdom is the sole focus of our recruitment activity. Unlike generalist agencies operating across multiple destinations, every resource, every process, and every institutional relationship within Aussie International Education is dedicated exclusively to UK higher education giving our university partners a depth of market knowledge and operational focus that multi-destination agencies cannot match.
We have developed a thorough, practical understanding of the UK higher education
landscape encompassing university structures, program offerings, entry requirements, CAS procedures, Student Route visa frameworks, and UKVI compliance obligations. This means we do not simply send applications; we send students who have been accurately assessed, properly prepared, and genuinely suited to their chosen institution and program.
Over the years, we have built strong, established relationships with a network of UK universities and colleges across foundation, undergraduate and postgraduate.
These relationships are grounded in trust, transparency, and a shared commitment to admissions integrity and we work to maintain them through consistent referral quality, intake after intake.
For UK universities seeking a recruitment partner in Nepal, we offer something straightforward: exclusive focus, compliance-first operations, and a reliable pipeline of well-prepared students managed professionally from first enquiry through to enrolment.
