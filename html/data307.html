About Us
Welcome to Collins GRE!

Located at Putalisadak in the heart of Kathmandu, Collins GRE Test Prep stands as a beacon of excellence in academic advancement. Our seasoned mentors possess a proven track record of success across all sections of the GRE - verbal, quantitative, and analytical writing - providing unparalleled quality in test preparation. With a diverse arsenal of effective teaching strategies, Collins consistently nurtures top scorers, cementing its reputation as the premier choice for GRE preparation.

Beyond GRE mastery, Collins offers comprehensive support to our students. We provide attentive assistance in crafting impeccable academic CVs, compelling statements of purpose, and invaluable mentorship for placements at esteemed American universities for both master’s and doctoral programs. Through personalized guidance and a commitment to navigating the intricate admission landscape, Collins empowers students with the tools and expertise essential for academic and professional triumph.

With Collins GRE Test Prep, your path to success is meticulously paved, ensuring a transformative journey towards your academic aspirations.
