Mars Education Consultant, a bridge for abroad study aspirants, is Ministry of Education and National Education Consultancies’ Association certified organization that offers various services including career counseling, documentation and visa application guidelines, and test preparation classes.We, hereby working as a bridge between students’ dream and reality, offer students the prospects to focus on their particular specializations and research that are not available in home country so that they will ultimately hone intellect and shape themselves into global citizen.

Mars Education Consultant envisages three departmental schools namely: Mars Language School offering all language classes, Mars Education School conducting training and workshops, and Mars Test Preparation School offering all language and aptitude mock tests and specialized guidelines. With three different separately located departmental schools, Mars aims to facilitate and guide students the best.

Career Counseling

Mars Education Consultant provides students an opportunity to select realistic program and courses to study in any country. This consultant tries to grasp the students’ perspective on where they want to study and proceeds with an observation of their background and academic performance. By understanding their background we can give them appropriate guidance. We prioritize a student’s choice, reason and motivation rather than promoting any college or university.

 

Ways of MARS Counseling

  • Counseling students using solution focused and person centered approaches by addressing issues such as academic performance, social skills and self-esteem.
  • Educating students about career awareness, university options and scholarships.
  • Promoting services through presentations on campus groups and classes and conducting career workshops.
  • Assistance with online recruiting system on campus recruiting, job listing and campus employment.

Alongside this, a professional team works hard to provide qualitative Test preparation service.
