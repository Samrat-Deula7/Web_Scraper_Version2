The Third Eye Journey

Established in 1998 by entrepreneur Anuj Paliwal, Third Eye Education Pvt. Ltd. is a leading institute for computer education and digital skills in North East India. Headquartered in Guwahati, the company has been shaping careers for over two decades through practical and industry-relevant training programs. Alongside its computer courses, Third Eye Education also runs Kid Veda (www.kidveda.com), an innovative early childhood learning initiative. Together, these ventures reflect the company’s vision to empower learners of all ages with knowledge, technology, and values for a brighter future.

Empowering Through Education

  • First ISO 9001:2008 certified Education Company in the region.
  • Largest network of training centers (Over 130) in the region.
  • First regional company selected by the Govt. of Assam to impart ICT Education in 120 Government Schools and Madrasas.
  • Experience of having trained over 4,50,000 students, till date.
  • Placed over 45,000 students both within and outside the region.
  • Registered under STPI & Exporting software through its subsidiary in the USA (State of South Carolina)
  • Member of trade bodies such as CII, FINER, NEITA, ICC etc.
  • Empaneled by NEC, Govt. of India to impart Employment Oriented Training to unemployed youth of North East India.
  • Students involved in both national and international live projects through the software development division of the company.
