Our Showcase

Our NCLEX program and features are showcased below.

NCLEX Intakes

There is no specific intake for NCLEX preparation. Every day can be a new intake. You can join our classes any time you wish to. We have all provisions to start new class every day.

NCLEX Online Classes

NCLEX online classes (first time in Asia) are getting more popular and effective than physical classes. Students can get live classes, recorded live classes that can be watched later, get resources online and give online tests.

NCLEX Class Timings

We have 4 shifts for NCLEX. Each shift has a duration of at least 2 hours without any interval. Topic wise tests and weekly tests are arranged accordingly. Currently we have 8-10 Am, 10-12 AM, 2-4 PM and 4-6 PM shifts.

NCLEX Course Duration

Duration fairly depends upon the student. If you are able to attend classes regularly, the minimum duration is 3 months and maximum up to 4 months. We conduct classes on a regular basis and no government holidays.

NCLEX Eligibility

PCL nurse or above with Nepali Nursing License can apply for NCLEX. There is no clinical experience or English test like IELTS or TOEFL is needed to appear for the NCLEX exam.

NCLEX fee Structures

Fee structures are not disclosed in our website. You have to contact our office. Apart from tuition fees, there are other parts that you should consider. So please contact us for the details about expenses.

CGFNS Processing & Documentation from Nepal

CGFNS processing can take up to 3 months - 6 months with charges depending upon the states. Board apply 1-2 months . PEARSON is completed within 48 hours for ATTN. After ATTN you can book for a NCLEX exam date.

English Requirement

English test score is very important for job placement in USA. So it should be taken as seriously as NCLEX. You can give eiter IELTS (7 in speaking with 6.5 each band) or TOEFL (84 total with 26 in speaking).

IELTS Mock Test

We are also Nepal's first Mock Test Center (www.mocktestcenter.com) for IELTS, TOEFL, SAT, GRE, GMAT. Our students get unlimited parctice at mock test lab with real exam environment. Book your mock test date.

Job Placement after NCLEX

After you pass NCLEX and complete English test score then we move forward for job placement in USA. For that there are several steps are involved. For detailed counseling about job placement please visit our office.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which institute is best for NCLEX in Nepal?
NPCN is Nepal's oldest and best NCLEX preparation center in Nepal, NPCN has given service to more than 7000+ nurses. NPCN is world's first company to develop NCLEX mock tests and assessment tools. Classes are online. NPCN has maintained one of the world's highest NCLEX pass rates.
Who can join NCLEX?
Any nurse having PCL or above can join our NCLEX classes.
What documents are required for NCLEX?
Just to join our NCLEX classes, no documents are required. The documents required for CGFNS and other processing will be fully guided by our expert staff after you join our NCLCE classes.
What is the duration for whole process?
Duration for NCLEX preparation is about 3-4 months. For whloe processing it may take up to 1 year. Please visit our office for further information.
Is IELTS or English test score required?
English is as essential as NCLEX. In IELTS, at least 7 in speaking with band not less than 6.5 is mendatory for occupational visa. For dependent visa, there is no compulsion of IELTS still some states may ask for it. English test score should be taken as seriously as NCLEX exam. For more details, please visit our office or call us.
Does age matter for NCLEX?
Age doesn't matter for NCLEX. Any age group can apply for NCLEX and job in USA.
What is the charge for NCLEX preparation?
There are several areas that you have to pay. For detailed information please visit our office.
