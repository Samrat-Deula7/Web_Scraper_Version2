Welcome to the Passion International Education Pvt. Ltd.

At Passion International Education, we are committed to delivering high-quality Japanese language education that goes beyond vocabulary and grammar. Since our establishment in 2010, we have empowered countless students to connect meaningfully with Japan by combining language proficiency with rich cultural understanding.

Our programs are designed to provide engaging, interactive, and personalized learning experiences that cater to each student’s goals—whether you're preparing for JLPT exams, planning to study or work in Japan, or simply striving for conversational fluency. Our team of experienced and dedicated instructors offers step-by-step guidance, ensuring that you build a strong foundation and gain confidence in real-world communication.

With over a decade of experience and a passion for teaching, we are proud to be your trusted partner on the journey to mastering the Japanese language and immersing yourself in its unique culture.

Message from Director

Dear Students, Parents, and Well-wishers, It is with great joy and heartfelt warmth that I welcome you to Passion International Education. As the Managing Director, I take immense pride in being part of an institution that is not only a center for academic excellence but also a gateway to international opportunities, personal growth, and cultural enrichment. At Passion International Education, our mission is deeply rooted in the belief that language is not just a medium of communication but a powerful bridge that connects people, cultures, and ideas. We are dedicated to equipping our students with strong proficiency in the Japanese language while also instilling a deep understanding of Japan’s rich culture, traditions, work ethic, and values. Whether your goal is to pursue higher studies in Japan, build a professional career, or immerse yourself in a new culture, we are here to guide you every step of the way. Our highly experienced and passionate team of instructors, counselors, and staff are committed to delivering quality education through a structured and interactive learning approach. From the basics of Hiragana and Katakana to advanced JLPT preparation, our courses are designed to meet the diverse needs of our students. More than just language learning, we focus on developing critical soft skills, cross-cultural communication, and personal discipline—qualities that are essential for success in Japan and beyond. We believe that every student has the potential to shine, and it is our responsibility to nurture that potential with care, encouragement, and the right guidance. At Passion International Education, we foster a warm, inclusive, and supportive environment where students feel valued, motivated, and empowered to chase their dreams with confidence. I also encourage parents and guardians to take an active interest in their child’s educational journey with us. Your support and engagement are crucial in shaping their future. Together, we can help our students not only reach Japan but also thrive there as global citizens and contributors to their communities. Let this be the beginning of a transformative chapter in your life. Step forward with curiosity, dedication, and passion—and we will be by your side to support, inspire, and uplift you throughout the journey. Thank you for choosing Passion International Education. Welcome to our family. Warm regards, Prakash Mani Niraula Managing Director Passion International Education Pvt. Ltd., Tinkune-32, Kathmandu, Nepal
