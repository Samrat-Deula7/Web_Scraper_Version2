Welcome to Pride Abroad Studies

Pride Abroad Studies Pvt. Ltd., established in 2012, is a government-recognized company in Nepal, specializing in student visa processing and counseling for Australia, Canada, USA, New Zealand, UK & Denmark. Our services encompass expert guidance, meticulous documentation, and test preparation, fostering the aspirations of students seeking education in esteemed institutions. With strong affiliations with renowned universities and colleges in these countries, our reputation has swiftly grown, an outcome of the collaborative efforts of our team and students. Embrace our motto ‘Be proud to be a part of Pride,’ and join us today for exceptional counseling, ensuring your journey towards successful education in Australia, Canada, USA, New Zealand, UK & Denmark.
