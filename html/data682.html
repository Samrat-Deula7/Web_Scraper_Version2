General Question

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to become proficient in Japanese?

The length of time it takes to become proficient in Japanese varies depending on the individual’s goals, dedication, and learning style. However, our courses are designed to provide a comprehensive and immersive learning experience that will help students achieve their language learning goals in a timely manner.

What levels of Japanese do you offer?

We offer classes for all levels of Japanese, from beginner to advanced. Our experienced teachers will assess your level and recommend the appropriate class for you.

What teaching methods do you use?

We use a communicative approach to teaching Japanese, which focuses on developing students’ language skills through practical and interactive activities. Our teachers use a variety of materials, including textbooks, audio and visual resources, and authentic materials such as news articles and videos.

What is the hardest JLPT test?

The JLPT has five levels :N1, N2, N3, N4 and N5. The earliest level is N5 and the most difficult level is N1, N4 and N5 measure the level of understanding of basic Japanese mainly learned in class.

What is the cost of courses?

The cost of courses varies depending on the level and duration of the course. Please contact us for more information on pricing and payment options.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for eligible students. Please contact us for more information on our scholarship and discount programs.

How do I enroll in a class?

To enroll in a class, please contact us to schedule a placement test and consultation with one of our customer support team. They will assess your level and recommend the appropriate class for you. Once you have been placed in a class, you can register and pay for the course.

What is the salary of JLPT N1 for freshers in india?

Japanese Language Expert salary in India ranges between Rs 3.0 lakhs to Rs 12.2 lakhs with an average annual salary of Rs 6.0 Lakhs for freshers.
