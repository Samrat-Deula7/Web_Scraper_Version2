Who we are?

Global Central Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. is a leading educational consultancy firm dedicated to helping students achieve their dreams of studying abroad. With years of experience and a proven track record, we specialize in providing comprehensive guidance for students aspiring to study in Japan, Australia, and the United Kingdom.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge, resources, and support they need to pursue world-class education and build successful international careers.

Our Business

We provide end-to-end support for students including career counseling, university selection, application processing, visa documentation, language preparation, and post-arrival assistance.
