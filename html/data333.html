    Job Description

    Roles and responsibilities of this job

    Real World is committed to empowering individuals through education and skill development. We are seeking an experienced Education Counsellor to join our team. The ideal candidate will guide students in exam preparation and provide counseling for international education opportunities. This role offers the chance to make a meaningful impact on students’ futures within a supportive and collaborative environment.

    Key Responsibilities: 

    • Provide professional guidance to students interested in studying abroad, particularly in Australia and EU countries, including course selection, university options, admission requirements, documentation, SOP preparation, and visa guidance.
    • Support students in planning their educational pathways and career goals, offering motivation, mentorship, and academic advice.
    • Prepare and deliver engaging lessons for IELTS and PTE exam preparation, ensuring students understand the latest exam formats, scoring criteria, and test-taking strategies.
    • Conduct mock tests, monitor performance, and provide personalized feedback to help students improve their English language skills.
    • Inspire and motivate students to achieve their academic and examination goals, combining both counseling and instructional support.

    Join us at Real World and play a pivotal role in shaping the educational journeys of aspiring learners. Your expertise will help pave the way for their success on a global stage.

    Job Specification

    Required qualifications for this job

    Required Education Level:Under Graduate (Bachelor)
    Required Experience:More than 2 years

    Other Specification

    • Bachelor’s degree in Education or a related field (required).
    • Minimum 2 years of experience as an Education Counsellor or IELTS/PTE Instructor.
    • Strong knowledge of current IELTS and PTE formats, scoring, and preparation techniques.
    • Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
    • Ability to motivate, mentor, and support students to achieve their academic goals.
    • Professional, student-centred, and committed to continuous improvement.
