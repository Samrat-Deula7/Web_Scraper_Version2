About Us

Eminent Educational Institute (EEI) was founded in November 13, 2003 with a vision to provide the highest quality, convenient and accessible service to individuals seeking high quality education abroad. It is operated by professional counselors who have outstanding, specific knowledge about universities located in particular countries. 

Objectives:

- To enable students to easily find universities which provide the highest quality of education according to the students’ financial affordability.

- To enable students to find universities providing scholarships and different kinds of financial aids.

- To enable students to easily understand the student visa application process to the particular countries.

- To provide information about the culture, rules and regulations of the particular countries.

- To provide very effective, efficient and score oriented online test preparation classes in SAT, TOEFL, GMAT, GRE and IELTS
