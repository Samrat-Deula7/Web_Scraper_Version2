About Us

SHIN EDUPOWER PVT. LTD. is an education company from Japan that has been developing and providing various unique and innovative educational programs for schools in India. Our programs have been successfully implemented into numerous schools in India, including top rank prestigious schools, receiving fantastic feedback from not only teachers and students, but principals and parents too.

Our Program

We offer a diverse range of programs designed to foster the holistic development of children and enhance the 21st-century skills essential for today’s world. Our programs are currently integrated into the curriculum of numerous prestigious schools across India.

 

<Our Programs>

  • Shin Program (Japanese-style experiential holistic learning / for KG to Class 5)
  • International Inquiry-Based Learning Program (Gateway to international programs / for Class 6 to 12 students)
  • Global Collaborative Program (Online-based international project learning / for Class 6 to 12 students)
  • Educational Visit to/from Japan ( cross-cultural immersion through direct interaction / for Class 7 to 12)
  • Japan Visit for Educators (for school managements and teachers)
