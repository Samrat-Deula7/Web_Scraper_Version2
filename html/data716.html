Since 2008, Boomerang International Educational Services has been a trusted partner for students aspiring to study abroad. With offices in Sri Lanka, Nepal, India, Kenya, Malaysia, and Hong Kong, we connect you to top universities and colleges across Australia, the UK, Canada, New Zealand, Ireland, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

What countries can Boomerang International help me study in? +
We assist students in pursuing education opportunities in Australia, New Zealand, Ireland, the UK, Germany, Canada, the USA, Dubai, Malta, Malaysia, and more.
How do I start my study abroad application with Boomerang? +
Simply contact us for a free consultation. Our expert counselors will guide you through course selection, university options, and the application process.
Does Boomerang offer scholarship guidance? +
Yes, we help students find and apply for scholarships, bursaries, and financial aid available in various countries and universities.
Can Boomerang assist with student visa applications? +
Absolutely! We provide comprehensive visa support, including document preparation, application submission, and interview coaching.
Are the universities Boomerang works with officially recognized? +
Yes. We partner with accredited universities and colleges worldwide that offer recognized qualifications.
What support do I get after arriving at my study destination? +
Boomerang provides pre-departure briefings and ongoing support, including accommodation advice, orientation help, and assistance with local settling-in.
Can I apply through Boomerang if I don’t meet the English language requirements? +
Yes, we can recommend pathway programs and English language courses to help you meet the requirements.
Is there a fee to use Boomerang’s services? +
Our counselling and application assistance services are free of charge. We are committed to helping you without hidden fees.
How long does the entire application process take? +
Application timelines vary depending on the country and university but typically range from 4 to 12 weeks from initial application to visa approval.
What makes Boomerang different from other education agents? +
Boomerang offers personalized, end-to-end support—from course selection and application to visa assistance and post-arrival care—ensuring a smooth and successful study abroad journey tailored to your unique needs.
