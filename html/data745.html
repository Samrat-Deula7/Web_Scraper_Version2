About Us

Broad Brain (B & B) Nepal Pvt. Ltd. located in Kathmandu-31, New Baneshwor, Nepal is a professional organization (Approved by the Ministry of Education, Nepal), which has been providing consultancy services in the field of education and career counselling from 2017 A.D. Since its inauguration, B & B Nepal has been working as an authorized admission partner with many Institutions globally (Especially in Japan) preserving very good partnerships under the uppermost standards of Business ethics and code of conduct for the recruitment of a good number of students every year.

Why B & B Nepal?

B & B Nepal is a trusted admission partner, especially for Japan, providing expert counseling, visa support, and top-quality language training with native instructors.

  • Career Counselling for study in Japan.
  • School/University selection assistance.
  • Complete Visa Processing & Guidance.
  • Language Classes: NAT, JLPT, JFT, J-Test, JLCT & J-Cert
  • SSW Skill test classes are available.
  • TIT preparation classes are running.
  • Experience and well-qualified instructor.
  • Japanese language taught by a Japanese teacher.
  • Japanese Culture and Manner classes.
  • Taught language on both Physical and online platforms.
