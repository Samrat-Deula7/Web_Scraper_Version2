Empowering Students Since 2005: Oasis Abroad Study Services

Introducing Oasis Abroad Study Services & Consultancy:Established in 2005 as Oasis Institute and later transitioning to Oasis Abroad Study Services & Consultancy, we have been a trusted name in the education sector in Nepal. Approved by the Ministry of Industry, Commerce & Supplies, Government of Nepal, our company is dedicated to providing quality services in the field of international education.

With a focus on career counseling, unbiased visa information, and test preparation classes, Oasis is committed to guiding deserving candidates towards their educational aspirations. Our journey began in Hetauda, the industrial hub of Nepal, before expanding to Kathmandu, the capital city, to broaden our reach and opportunities globally
