    Welcome to Dynamic Universe

    Dynamic Universe Education Plus Immigration Services Pvt. Ltd. (formerly America Computer Institute), established in 1998, has been a beacon of opportunity for Nepali students seeking quality education abroad. Headquartered in Putalisadak Chowk, Kathmandu, with additional offices in Pokhara, Bhaktapur, Australia, and Dubai, Dynamic Universe assists over 200+ students annually in securing placements at distinguished universities and colleges across Asia, Europe, Australia, and North America.

      Faq's

      Common Frequently Asked Questions

      Which countries and universities are suitable for my chosen field of study?

      We can help you explore universities renowned for your field in countries like the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Japan and more. It depends on your preferences, budget, and the specific program you are interested in.

      What is the application process, and how can I start it?

      We will assist you in preparing all necessary documents, guide you through the online application process, and ensure you meet all deadlines. The process often involves submitting transcripts, test scores, recommendation letters, and a personal statement.

      What are the language proficiency requirements, and how can I meet them?

      Language proficiency requirements vary, but commonly accepted tests include IELTS, PTE or TOEFL. We can help you prepare for these tests and provide resources to enhance your language skills.

      What is the duration of the application process, and when should I start preparing?

      Application timelines vary, but generally, it’s advisable to start the process at least a year before the intended start date. This allows time for test preparation, document gathering, and completing the application.

      Are there any scholarships or financial aid options available for international students?

      Yes, many universities offer scholarships, and there are external scholarship opportunities. We will assist you in identifying and applying for scholarships that match your profile.

      What is the cost of living in the country I’m planning to study in?

      We can provide you with an estimate of the cost of living, including accommodation, food, transportation, and other expenses, based on the location of your chosen university.
