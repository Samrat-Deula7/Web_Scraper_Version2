About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Educational Success

Coastline Education, an independent student placement agency in Nepal, has been dedicated to empowering Nepalese students for accessing education in Australia and other countries since 2015.


We are now officially collaborating with more than 200 higher institutions in Australia and other countries, and a number of others unofficially depending on students’ academic interests, financial strengths, and other parameters.

Our Solutions

Top-Tier Educational Services

At our consultancy, we offer a range of tailored services designed to guide students through every step of their educational journey.
