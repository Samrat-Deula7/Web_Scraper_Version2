    welcome to our college

    Novel Academy is accredited with the University of Cambridge International Examinations, UK, the British Council, Nepal, and Ministry of Education and Sports, Nepal to offer University of Cambridge International GCE AS and A Level courses and is affiliated to Purbanchal University to offer MBA, BBA, B.Pharmacy and BSc.Nursing Programs in Pokhara.

    course categories

    MBA

    The MBA program at Novel GSM emphasizes strategic thinking and leadership: why knowledge is significant and how to apply learning to drive results.

    BBA

    The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) at Novel Academy is a four-year, 129 credit-hour program of Purbanchal University.The program aims to encourage students to be confident and competent in the modern world.

    BSC.NURSING

    Graduate School of Nursing, Novel Academy offers four year BSc. Nursing program which prepares competent B.Sc. nurses to function independently in multidisciplinary health fields to provide care to the needy people of the community regarding the preventive and rehabilitative aspects of health.

    M. Pharm (Clinical Pharmacy)

    B.PHARMACY

    Novel Graduate School of Pharmacy offers Bachelor of Pharmacy (B Pharm) an undergraduate degree with a combination of biology and chemistry. The study of pharmacy is mainly concerned with the action of drugs on biological systems and their applications for human drug therapy.
