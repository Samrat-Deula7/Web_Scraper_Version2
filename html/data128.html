ABOUT US

Orient Consultancy Pvt. Ltd.

Orient Consultancy Pvt. Ltd., established in 2014, is a trusted educational consultancy located beside PK Campus in Bagbazar, Kathmandu. We are dedicated to guiding Nepalese students toward global academic opportunities with a focus on transparency, reliability, and results. Our core strength lies in student visa processing for countries such as South Korea, Australia, Japan, the USA, Germany, and other European nations. Among these, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high visa success rates, especially for South Korea, thanks to our direct institutional ties and experienced team.

From university selection and application support to visa documentation and interview preparation, we provide complete guidance at every step. We also offer expert counselling, language classes, and support for skilled migration pathways, helping individuals pursue both education and career opportunities abroad.

With a student-first approach and commitment to honest service, Orient Consultancy Nepal has become a preferred choice for those seeking to study or settle overseas.

SERVICES

Expert Counselling

We provide personalized counselling sessions to help you choose the right country, university, and course based on your goals, academic background, and budget. Our team stays updated with the latest visa policies and educational opportunities.

Study Visa Processing

Our experienced team handles your application and documentation process efficiently. We have a proven track record of successful visa applications, especially for South Korea, Australia, Japan, USA, Germany, and other European countries.

Skill Immigration Assistance

Looking to build your career abroad? We also offer support and guidance for skilled migration pathways, helping eligible individuals apply for work permits or permanent residency in countries like Germany and Australia.

Interview Preparation & Guidance

We provide regular interview preparation classes, mock interviews, and one-on-one coaching sessions to ensure you’re fully confident and ready for embassy interviews or university admissions.

Language Classes

We offer professional coaching for essential international languages and tests.

Regular Mock Tests

We conduct weekly mock tests to monitor your progress and improve your performance, especially for language exams like IELTS, PTE, and university entrance exams.
