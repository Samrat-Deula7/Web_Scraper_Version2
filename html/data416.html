Message from Board

"Namaste and Konnichiwa !! We would like to heartily welcome all the students to Waseda Japanese Language School, who are eager to learn Japanese Language in order to study in Japan for their further education and also those whom are trying to switch to Japan as a worker, dependent, trainee worker and with any other visas. Actually, we have created this platform to enhance your Japanese language skills and so that you can cope with the advanced education system of Japan and also can make a comfortable adjustment in the workplaces."

Basically, We want our students and learners to have a good proficiency in Japanese Language so that they can have access to any new courses they wish to learn. We also want our students to completely be dedicated in their studies and work, learn and have expertise in their field and, ultimately, begin their own life career in Nepal, their homeland, out of which they have gained boosting up with new vocational skills and technology from Japan. We believe, this will be a great sacrifice and contribution for both countries Japan and Nepal. More importantly, learning Japanese hospitality, manners, culture and discipline is very crucial asset in your life. Waseda Japanese Language School can be a new beginning to connect your further study and life career. We have veteran Japanese Language Instructors and Consultants; you can visit us in your comfortable time and get benefit of it.
