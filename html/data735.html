“Where Diversity Resonates— A Bridge 

Connecting the Local Community and the World”

 Founded in 1989 with the support and cooperation of local educational institutions,
our school was established with the vision of becoming a Japanese language school that contributes to the vitality of the local community.

Since then, we have worked closely with learners from diverse cultural backgrounds,
fostering strong connections with the surrounding community over many years.

Looking ahead, we will continue to stay rooted in the local area while serving as a place for the practice of multicultural coexistence.
By responding flexibly to the changing times, we aim to further expand the possibilities of education.
