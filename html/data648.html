ABOUT US

Welcome to AHTI

Air Hostess Training Institutes (AHTI) is the 1st institute to provide Cabin Crew (Air Hostess & Flight Steward) Training in Nepal, established on 25th August 2003. AHTI is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, which is the only one and the first to achieve an ISO (International Standard Organization) certification award for its quality and services in the field of Cabin Crew Training in Nepal, as well AHTI is also accredited by International Accreditation Organization (IAO),USA. AHTI is established not only by Aviation professionals but by Aviation Enthusiasts! We are committed to the Aviation Industry and to the development of our students who have dreams of being a part of this growing airline Industry. We are in aviation because we enjoy the challenges and rewards that it brings. We at AHTI aim to offer Professional Services to our fellow Professionals in the field. AHTI takes pride in being the pioneer institutes in providing professional trainings to young girls and boys who have a high ambition of working high up in the sky.
