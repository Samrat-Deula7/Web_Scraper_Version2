    V V College of Engineering

    V V College of Engineering is a brain child of our beloved Chairman Shri S Vaikundarajan and it was in his mind for a long time, and took shape only the year 2010. V V College of Engineering is situated in the southern part of the East Coast of India. This college comes under the jurisdiction of Anna University.

    V V Minerals

    V V Mineral (V V M) is India's largest Mining, Manufacturer and Exporter of Garnet and Ilmenite. At the global level, we are poised to rise above our present number two position. V V M is the first private Ilmenite Exporter in India. Established in 1989, we have achieved significant market share in Europe, Middle East, East Asia, Australia and the USA.

    V V Pigments

    V V Titanium Pigments Pvt. Ltd is a chemical manufacturing unit of V V Group, a family run business with group turnover of 1450 crores. V V Ti is the leading Indian manufacturer and exporter of Titanium dioxide under the brand of OpaTi and Ferrous sulphate heptahydrate as by product.

    V V Rice Mill

    V V Rice Mill is a distinguished manufacturer and supplier of a variety of rice. Our association with the Rice Industry is from the past three decades, under the brand name PR. We hold an eminent position in Rice Market owing to the quality we maintain.

    V V Paiint

    V V Paiint is the newest forward integration venture from V V Mineral (V V M) and has been launched with a state-of-art manufacturing facility for Industrial & Decorative Coatings.
