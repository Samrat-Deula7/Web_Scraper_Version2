ABOUT US

Speed International (P.) Ltd., an education consultancy established in 2004, registered under the company act, 2053 BS with registration no. 27278/060/61, is located at Putalisadak, Kathmandu, Nepal. We provide proper counseling and enrollment services to Nepalese students who are seeking admission into different renowned Universities in China. Being South Asian Representative of Chinese Universities we have also overseas associates in India, Bangladesh, Pakistan & China. Speed is a member of the Education Consultancy Association of Nepal (ECAN) since its establishment and is approved by the Ministry of Education, Government of Nepal to counsel the students.

 Now, students have so many options to choose medical educations globally and among them, China is one of the best destinations in quality and comparatively cost-effective. As per our experience, Chinese effective rules and regulations make the study best and safe stay during the entire study period. Further, you may find multiple consultancies working for Medical education in the market, but choosing the right and experience institute is very important to achieve your goal. Our history, satisfied students, graduated doctors as well parents support show the facts about Speed.
