MESSAGE FROM

DIRECTOR

MRS. SAFALA TAMRAKAR

For the past 19 years, Milestone Consult Services has been dedicated to delivering top-notch counseling services in the realm of studying abroad. We take immense pride in the success stories we've achieved. With over a thousand consultancies operating in Nepal, it's gratifying to note that our former students, who availed themselves of our services, highly recommend us.

Beyond just counseling for overseas studies, we also extend our support to guidance in documentation , ensuring we meet the diverse needs of our students. Collaborating with both national and international educational sectors, we strive to offer our students and clients high-quality services.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our continuous efforts to expand our network of educational institutions, providing a wider array of choices to our students. As a registered and authorized company endorsed by the Education Ministry of Nepal, and a proud member of the Education Consultancy Association of Nepal (ECAN), we are fully committed to upholding the highest standards.

We express our heartfelt gratitude to our dedicated staff, educational providers and well-wishers. It is their unwavering support that has propelled us to this height of success. To our new students, we pledge to continue offering quality services at affordable rates.

Thank you for entrusting us with your educational journey.
