About Us

Introduction

Emerald Education Foundation (EEF) is one of the finest education institutes in Kathmandu, Nepal which properly guides you with your education solutions in and outside various countries.EEF was founded in 2015 and commenced its operation under the name of Emerald Foundation which was later transformed into Emerald Education Foundation (EEF). With the help of Experienced and Qualified  Education Counsellors and founders, Emerald Education has proved itself as one of the best educational institutes in the town for study-related services.

Being an institution with a long history, EEF has been providing the best career guidance and assures that every student is equally competent and all set to welcome new challenges in their career path globally. In essence, Emerald Education Foundation can be your reliable team to find easy solutions for all of your study application confusions.
